## Escalation Triggers
Escalate to Marketplace Seller Support if:
- Seller misses response SLA
- Repeated defective-item complaints on same SKU in short window
- Seller and customer dispute item condition with conflicting evidence

## Operational Flags

- Fast seller response performance historically, but accessory packing omissions have been observed on specific SKUs (see quality history docs)
