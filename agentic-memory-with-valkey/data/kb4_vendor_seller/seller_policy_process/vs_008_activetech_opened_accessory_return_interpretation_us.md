# ActiveTech Opened Electronics Accessory Return Interpretation (US)

## Purpose
Clarify seller-specific handling for opened electronics accessories sold by ActiveTech Market.

## Scope
Seller-fulfilled ActiveTech Market orders in US region.

## Seller Interpretation Summary
ActiveTech may deny "changed mind" returns for opened accessories in specific categories (e.g., opened adhesive mounts, opened hygiene-sensitive accessories, certain sealed electrical components) unless item is defective or materially not as described.

## Agent Handling Guidance
- Confirm return reason (changed mind vs defective vs wrong item vs not as described)
- Do not quote retailer default opened-item policy for seller-fulfilled ActiveTech orders
- If customer reports defect or safety issue, use defect/safety claim flow, not remorse return flow

## Customer Communication Guidance
Use factual, neutral wording:
- "Because this item was sold and fulfilled by the marketplace seller, their return policy may differ from retailer-fulfilled items."
- "If the item is defective or not as described, we can help route that claim under the seller and marketplace protections."

## Escalation Triggers
- Safety complaint (heat, sparks, smoke, burn risk)
- Seller denies defect claim without review evidence
- Repeated complaint pattern on same SKU category
