# GearVault Outlet Renewed Product Support Boundaries

## Purpose
Define support boundaries for Accent Athletics products purchased from GearVault Outlet (GVO-US-618) in "Renewed" or "Open Box" condition.

## Warranty Summary
| Issue Type | Who Handles | Warranty |
|---|---|---|
| Hardware defect (physical) | GearVault | 90-day GearVault warranty |
| Firmware / software issue | Accent Athletics | Full support regardless of source |
| App connectivity | Accent Athletics | Full support |
| Cosmetic issue (scratch, scuff) | Nobody — sold as-is | Open box items may have cosmetic wear |
| Missing accessories | GearVault | Must be reported within 7 days of delivery |
| Return / exchange | GearVault | 30-day window (vs 60-day direct) |

## Key Principle
**Software support is universal. Hardware support follows the seller.**

A SwiftPace watch from GearVault gets the same firmware updates, the same known-issue workarounds (ts_022 bricking fix, ts_017 GPS troubleshooting), and the same app support as a direct purchase. But if the watch's physical button breaks after 4 months, that's outside GearVault's 90-day warranty and outside Accent Athletics warranty (product was sold as renewed, not new).

## Common Scenarios

### "My renewed PulseFit Band 4 has the sync delay issue"
- Known issue ts_003/ts_004. Provide workaround. This is firmware, not hardware. Full support.

### "My open-box AeroFlex Runner 2 sole is separating after 3 weeks"
- Physical defect. Within GearVault's 90-day window → redirect to GearVault.
- If outside 90 days → no warranty coverage from either party. Customer accepted open-box condition.
- **Do NOT process through Accent Athletics warranty** — the product was not sold as new.

### "My renewed SwiftPace watch won't turn on"
- If within 90 days of purchase → GearVault DOA/defect replacement.
- If outside 90 days → no coverage. Offer Accent Athletics repair service at customer's cost ($79 flat rate for SwiftPace).

### "I didn't know it was open-box / renewed"
- GearVault listings clearly state condition. However, if customer genuinely didn't realize:
  - Within 30 days: GearVault accepts returns for any reason.
  - Outside 30 days: cannot override GearVault's policy. Empathize but explain.

## Price Matching
Customers sometimes ask why GearVault's price was lower than direct. Do NOT explain the liquidation model in detail. Simply say: "GearVault is an authorized outlet partner that sells open-box and renewed products at reduced prices. The trade-off is a shorter warranty period."
