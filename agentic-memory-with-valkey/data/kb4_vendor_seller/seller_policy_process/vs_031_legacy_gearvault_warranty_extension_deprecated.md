# Legacy GearVault Warranty Extension Promotion (Deprecated)
**Status:** Deprecated

> **Status: DEPRECATED** | Last updated: 2026-01-31
> **Reason:** Promotion ended. GearVault renewed products now carry standard 90-day GearVault warranty only.

## Original Content (Do Not Use)
During Q4 2025 holiday season (Nov 15 – Dec 31, 2025), GearVault ran a promotional "Extended Warranty" offer:
- All renewed electronics purchased during the promo period received a 180-day warranty (instead of standard 90-day)
- Promotion was funded by GearVault, not Accent Athletics

## Why Deprecated
The promotion ended Dec 31, 2025. Products purchased during the promo period still have the 180-day warranty (through Jun 30, 2026 at latest), but:
- **New purchases after Dec 31, 2025 have standard 90-day warranty**
- Agents should NOT promise 180-day warranty on current GearVault purchases

## How to Check
If customer claims they have the extended warranty:
- Check order date. If Nov 15 – Dec 31, 2025: they may be correct. Verify with GearVault.
- If order date is 2026: standard 90-day warranty applies. The promotion is over.

## Common Mistake
Some agents see this document and tell customers "GearVault offers 180-day warranty on electronics." This is NO LONGER TRUE for new purchases. The current warranty is 90 days per vs_019.
