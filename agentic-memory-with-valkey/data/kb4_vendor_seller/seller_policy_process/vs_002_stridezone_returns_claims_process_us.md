# StrideZone Direct Returns and Claims Process (US)

## Purpose
Defines seller-specific return, missing-part, and damage claim handling for StrideZone Direct marketplace orders.

## Scope
Applies to:
- Seller-fulfilled StrideZone Direct orders in the US marketplace
- Contact-center and marketplace support handling

## Return Window (Seller Policy)
- Standard return request window: **30 calendar days** from delivery date
- Item must be returned in original condition unless damaged/defective claim applies
- Used outdoor gear may be denied under standard remorse return unless defect is documented

## Damage-on-Arrival (DOA) Claims
For damaged-item claims:
1. Confirm order is seller-fulfilled by StrideZone Direct
2. Request photos of item damage and packaging when available
3. Submit claim through marketplace seller claim workflow
4. Do not promise immediate refund before seller review unless platform policy requires override

## Missing Parts / Missing Accessories
- Customer should identify missing component (e.g., buckle, strap, lid)
- Photo of received contents recommended
- Seller may offer replacement component before full replacement/refund depending SKU

## Agent Guidance
- Use neutral phrasing: "The seller will review the claim under their marketplace policy."
- If seller misses SLA, escalate using marketplace seller escalation routing playbook
- Do not apply retailer in-store return instructions to seller-fulfilled StrideZone Direct orders

## Exceptions
Platform-level consumer protection and legal requirements may override seller policy. Check current marketplace policy/compliance guidance if dispute escalates.
