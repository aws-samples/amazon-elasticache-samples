# PeakForm Athletics Counterfeit Product Handling Playbook

## Purpose
Guide agents on handling customer contacts related to products purchased from suspended seller PeakForm Athletics (PFA-US-720).

## Background
PeakForm was suspended 2026-02-15 for selling counterfeit Accent Athletics products. See vs_015 for full details.

## Step-by-Step Agent Process

### Step 1: Identify PeakForm Purchase
- Check order details. Seller field will show "PeakForm Athletics" or seller ID "PFA-US-720."
- PeakForm listings have been removed from marketplace but order history remains.

### Step 2: Do NOT Troubleshoot the Product
- PeakForm products are not genuine Accent Athletics products.
- Firmware updates will not work (counterfeit electronics run different firmware).
- Sizing guides (KB1: pk_005) do not apply (counterfeit shoes have different construction).
- Known issues (KB3) do not apply.
- **If customer says "my AeroFlex Runner 2 from PeakForm is uncomfortable":** the shoe is not a real AeroFlex Runner 2. Do not consult the substitution playbook or fit guide.

### Step 3: Offer Refund
- All PeakForm orders from the last 90 days: automatic full refund.
- Process via Marketplace Refund Tool → Reason: "Seller Suspended — Counterfeit"
- Refund to original payment method. Processing time: 3–5 business days.
- Orders older than 90 days: escalate to Marketplace Seller Support for case-by-case review.

### Step 4: Offer Replacement Path
After processing refund, offer:
- "Would you like help finding the genuine version of the product you were looking for? I can help you purchase directly from Accent Athletics or from one of our authorized sellers."
- If customer wants the same product: direct to accentathletics.com or authorized sellers (RunReady for footwear/apparel, FitTech for electronics, TrailBound for trail gear).

### Step 5: Safety Warning for Electronics
- If customer purchased a "SwiftPace watch" or "AeroTrack earbuds" from PeakForm: these are counterfeit electronics with unknown safety certifications.
- Approved messaging: "For your safety, we recommend discontinuing use of the electronic device you received, as it was not manufactured by Accent Athletics and may not meet our safety and quality standards."
- Do NOT say "it might explode" or "it's dangerous" — we don't know the actual risk level. Keep it factual.

## Language Guidelines
- Use: "did not meet our authenticity standards"
- Use: "was not manufactured by Accent Athletics"
- Avoid: "counterfeit," "fake," "knockoff" (unless customer uses these words first)
- Avoid: any reference to legal proceedings
