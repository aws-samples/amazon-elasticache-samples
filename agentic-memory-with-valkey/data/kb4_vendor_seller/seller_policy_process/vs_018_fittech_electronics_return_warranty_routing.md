# FitTech Solutions Electronics Return & Warranty Routing Guide

## Purpose
Clarify the boundary between FitTech seller support and Accent Athletics product support for electronics purchased from FitTech Solutions (FTS-US-502).

## The Core Question: Who Handles What?

### FitTech Handles (Seller Issues)
- Wrong item shipped
- Item damaged in shipping (crushed box, cracked screen on arrival)
- Dead on arrival (DOA) — device won't power on out of box
- Missing accessories (no charging cable, no ear tips, etc.)
- Return/exchange requests (30-day window)
- Order never arrived / tracking shows delivered but customer didn't receive

### Accent Athletics Handles (Product Issues)
- Firmware bugs and updates (all known issues in KB3: ts_003, ts_019, ts_020, ts_021, ts_022)
- App connectivity issues (sync failures, pairing problems)
- Software features not working as expected (ANC, GPS, HR sensor, sleep tracking)
- Manufacturer warranty claims (hardware defect discovered after use — not DOA)
- Product how-to and setup guidance (all setup guides in KB3)

## Common Misrouting Scenarios

### Scenario 1: "My SwiftPace bricked during update"
Customer bought from FitTech. Watch bricked during firmware v3.2.0 update.
- **This is an Accent Athletics issue** (ts_022). FitTech did not cause this.
- Follow ts_022 recovery procedure. If recovery fails, Accent Athletics initiates warranty replacement — NOT FitTech.

### Scenario 2: "My AeroTrack left earbud dies fast"
Customer bought from FitTech. Left earbud battery drain on firmware v2.4.0.
- **This is an Accent Athletics issue** (ts_019). Provide workaround per ts_019.
- Do NOT redirect to FitTech.

### Scenario 3: "My StrideScale arrived with a cracked display"
Customer bought from FitTech. Scale display was cracked when unboxed.
- **This is a FitTech issue** (shipping damage / DOA).
- Redirect to FitTech: "Since this appears to be shipping damage, the seller FitTech Solutions will handle your replacement. I'll connect you with their support team."

### Scenario 4: "My PulseFit Band 4 won't pair — I bought it from FitTech"
- **Start with Accent Athletics troubleshooting** (ts_001, ts_005). Pairing issues are product issues.
- If troubleshooting reveals the device is DOA (won't power on at all): redirect to FitTech for replacement.

### Scenario 5: "FitTech sent me a SwiftPace watch without a charging cable"
- **FitTech issue.** Missing accessory from seller packaging.
- But note: FitTech bundles third-party accessories. Verify the "missing cable" is the Accent Athletics OEM cable and not a third-party bundle item. If it's the OEM cable, FitTech must ship replacement.

## Escalation
If unclear whether issue is seller or product: escalate to Marketplace Seller Support (vs_009 routing playbook) with notes on what you've determined so far. Do not bounce the customer between teams.
