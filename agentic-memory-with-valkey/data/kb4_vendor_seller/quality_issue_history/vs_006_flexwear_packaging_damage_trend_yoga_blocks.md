# FlexWear Collective Packaging Damage Trend (Yoga Blocks)

## Summary
Moderate increase in damage complaints reported for selected yoga block SKUs sold by FlexWear Collective, especially during high-volume shipping periods.

## Scope
- Seller: FlexWear Collective (FWC-US-204)
- Product family: Yoga blocks / fragile kitchen storage
- Region: US

## Pattern Indicators
- Cracks at lid rim or body edge
- Packaging appears intact externally in some cases
- Repeat complaints cluster around a small group of SKUs

## Agent Guidance
- Request photos of item and packaging
- Confirm fulfillment ownership before giving return instructions
- For repeat-SKU incidents, include quality-pattern tag in case notes
- Avoid blaming carrier or seller without documented evidence

## Monitoring Status
Pattern under review. No stop-sell instruction at this time.
