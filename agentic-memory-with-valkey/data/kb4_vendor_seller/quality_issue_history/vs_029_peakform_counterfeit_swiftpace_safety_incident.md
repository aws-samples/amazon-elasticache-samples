# PeakForm Athletics: Counterfeit SwiftPace Watch Safety Incident

## Summary
A customer who purchased a "SwiftPace Running Watch" from PeakForm Athletics (PFA-US-720, suspended) reported the device overheated during charging and melted the charging cable. No injury reported. Incident occurred Feb 10, 2026.

## Incident Details
- Customer purchased "SwiftPace Running Watch" from PeakForm on Jan 15, 2026 for $89.99 (genuine retail: $249.99 — price alone was a red flag)
- Device overheated during overnight charging on Feb 10
- Charging cable melted at the connection point
- Scorch mark on nightstand surface
- Customer unplugged immediately. No fire, no injury.
- Customer contacted Accent Athletics support believing it was a genuine product

## Investigation
- Product Safety team requested the device for inspection
- Confirmed: NOT a genuine SwiftPace watch. Internal components are generic smartwatch hardware with no UL/CE safety certification.
- Battery is an unbranded lithium polymer cell with no protection circuit — overcharge protection absent.
- This is consistent with the counterfeit pattern identified in PeakForm's suspension (vs_015).
