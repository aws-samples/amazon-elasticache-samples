# Atlanta Ponce City Market BeltLine Construction Advisory
**Store:** ATL-003 (Ponce City Market)
**Region:** US-GA
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-02-15 to 2026-04-30

## Situation
BeltLine Eastside Trail segment adjacent to Ponce City Market is under construction for trail widening project. Pedestrian detour in effect — trail users rerouted to N Ave sidewalk.

## Impact
- Walk-in traffic from BeltLine reduced ~40% during construction.
- Noise from construction equipment 7 AM–5 PM weekdays. Minimal weekend work.
- Curbside pickup on N Ave side unaffected.
- Vendor deliveries unaffected (use loading dock on Ponce de Leon Ave).

## Customer Communication
- Signage on N Ave: "BeltLine detour? We're still here! Enter from N Ave or Ponce de Leon Ave."
- Social media: post weekly BeltLine construction updates with store access info.

## Staffing Adjustment
- Reduce weekday floor staff by 1 during construction period. Maintain weekend staffing (construction paused).
- Reallocate saved hours to Saturday morning run club support.

## Expected Resolution
Atlanta BeltLine Inc estimates trail reopening by Apr 30. Monitor for delays.
