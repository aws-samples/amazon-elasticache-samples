# San Francisco Union Square Retail Theft Surge Advisory
**Store:** SFO-001 (Union Square)
**Region:** US-CA
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-02-20 to 2026-03-20

## Situation
SFPD has reported a 35% increase in organized retail theft in the Union Square area over the past 2 weeks. Multiple retailers on Post St and Geary St have been targeted.

## Enhanced Security Measures (Effective Immediately)
- **Two security guards** on duty during all open hours (up from one). Second guard funded by Regional Ops through Mar 20.
- **Fitting room policy tightened:** Max 3 items (down from 5). Associate must be stationed at fitting room entrance at all times.
- **High-value lockdown:** All items over $75 (down from $100) require associate retrieval. No self-service on premium footwear wall.
- **Bag check:** Offer to hold large bags/backpacks at register. Do not require — California law prohibits mandatory bag checks for non-employees.
- **Door greeter:** Station one associate at entrance during peak hours (11 AM–6 PM) as visible deterrent and to greet customers.

## Incident Response
- If theft occurs: do NOT chase or physically confront. Note description, direction of travel, and call SFPD (non-emergency: 415-553-0123) immediately.
- File internal incident report in StoreLink within 1 hour.
- If organized group (3+ individuals acting in coordination): call 911 immediately.

## Expected Resolution
SFPD has increased patrols in Union Square. Reassess security posture on Mar 20.
