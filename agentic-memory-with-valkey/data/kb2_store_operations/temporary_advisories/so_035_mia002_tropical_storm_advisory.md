# Miami Brickell Tropical Storm Watch Advisory
**Store:** MIA-002 (Brickell City Centre)
**Region:** US-FL
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-02-25 to 2026-02-28

## Situation
NWS has issued a Tropical Storm Watch for Miami-Dade County. Tropical Storm Elara expected to bring 50–65 mph winds and 4–8 inches of rain Feb 26–27.

## Pre-Storm Actions (Complete by 6 PM Feb 25)
- Secure all outdoor signage and displays.
- Move inventory away from windows and glass storefronts.
- Back up POS data to cloud.
- Charge all backup battery packs.
- Verify emergency kit: water (3-day supply for 6 people), first aid, flashlights, battery radio.

## Closure Decision
- If NWS upgrades to Tropical Storm Warning: close store. Store Manager does not need Regional Ops approval.
- If watch expires without upgrade: resume normal operations.
- Mall management may independently decide to close Brickell City Centre — follow their directive.

## Post-Storm Reopening
- Do NOT reopen until Store Manager completes safety walkthrough.
- Check for water intrusion, broken glass, structural damage.
- Test all electrical systems before powering on POS.
- Follow reopening checklist in so_010 (Emergency Closure Playbook).

## Customer Communication
- Pending pickup orders: auto-extend hold timer. Send notification: "Due to weather conditions, your pickup at Brickell City Centre is temporarily on hold. We'll notify you when the store reopens."
- Suppress store from online pickup options when closure decision is made.
