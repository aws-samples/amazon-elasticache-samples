# Denver Cherry Creek Pipe Burst Closure Advisory
**Store:** DEN-021 (Denver Cherry Creek)
**Region:** US-CO
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-02-23 to 2026-03-02

## Situation
Sprinkler pipe burst in stockroom overnight Feb 22 due to freezing temperatures. Water damage to stockroom floor and ~15% of back stock. Retail floor unaffected but store closed for remediation.

## Current Status
- **Store is CLOSED** as of Feb 23.
- ServPro remediation crew on-site. Industrial dehumidifiers running 24/7.
- Insurance adjuster scheduled Feb 25.
- Damaged inventory being cataloged for write-off.

## Order Handling
- All pending pickups transferred to nearest store: Accent Athletics Park Meadows (DEN-045, 12 miles south). Customers notified via email/SMS with new pickup location.
- Ship-from-store orders rerouted to DEN-045 via StoreLink.
- Store suppressed from online pickup and ship-from-store.

## Staffing
- Store Manager and 1 associate on-site daily for remediation oversight.
- Remaining staff temporarily reassigned to DEN-045 and Boulder store (BLD-001).

## Reopening Estimate
- Best case: Mar 1 (if remediation completes on schedule and no mold found).
- Worst case: Mar 7 (if mold remediation required).
- Regional Ops will issue reopening advisory 24 hours before store reopens.
