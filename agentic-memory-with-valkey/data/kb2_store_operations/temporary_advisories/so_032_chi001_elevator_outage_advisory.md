# Chicago Michigan Ave Elevator Outage Advisory
**Store:** CHI-001 (Michigan Ave Flagship)
**Region:** US-IL
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-02-25 to 2026-03-01

## Situation
Passenger elevator serving floors 1–3 is out of service for motor replacement. Freight elevator operational but not for customer use.

## Impact
- Customers must use stairs to access floors 2 and 3.
- ADA accommodation: bring merchandise from upper floors to ground floor fitting area for customers who cannot use stairs. Dedicate one associate per shift to this role.
- Stroller-friendly: same as ADA — bring items down. Do not attempt to carry strollers up stairs.

## Customer Communication
- Signage at elevator: "Elevator temporarily out of service. Our team is happy to bring any item to the ground floor for you — just ask!"
- No change to online messaging needed (pickup is ground floor).

## Expected Resolution
Elevator company (Otis) estimates repair complete by Mar 1. Parts are on-site.
