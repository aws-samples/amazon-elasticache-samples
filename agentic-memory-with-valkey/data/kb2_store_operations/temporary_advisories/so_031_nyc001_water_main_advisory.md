# NYC SoHo Water Main Break Advisory
**Store:** NYC-001 (SoHo Flagship)
**Region:** US-NY
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-02-24 to 2026-02-28

## Situation
NYC DEP is repairing a water main on Broadway between Prince and Spring. Construction barriers partially block store entrance. Restrooms unavailable due to water shutoff.

## Impact
- Store entrance reduced to single door (south side). North entrance blocked by barriers.
- Restrooms closed. Direct customers to Starbucks at 131 Spring St (1 block).
- Vendor deliveries rerouted to Crosby St freight entrance only.
- Noise from jackhammers 8 AM–4 PM. Consider offering earplugs to fitting room customers (stockroom has a box).

## Customer Communication
- Signage at entrance: "Pardon our dust! Water main repair in progress. Full store access through south entrance."
- Online pickup messaging: "Note: Our SoHo store entrance is temporarily modified due to street construction. Enter via the south door on Broadway."

## Expected Resolution
NYC DEP estimates completion by Feb 28. If extended, Regional Ops will issue updated advisory.
