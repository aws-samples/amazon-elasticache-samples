# Portland Pearl District Weather Service Advisory
**Store:** PDX-008 (Portland Pearl District)  
**Region:** US-OR  
**Status:** Active  
**Effective:** 2026-02-22 to 2026-02-25

## Summary
Due to severe weather conditions and intermittent street access restrictions, curbside service at PDX-008 is temporarily suspended.

## What Is Affected
- Curbside pickup: Suspended
- In-store pickup: Available with reduced staffing
- In-store returns: Available; expect longer wait times

## Agent Guidance
- Inform customers curbside is temporarily unavailable
- Offer in-store pickup if customer can safely travel
- Do not provide reopen time beyond effective advisory window unless updated note is issued

## Reinstatement
Curbside service will resume when weather and access conditions improve and store leadership confirms safe operations.
