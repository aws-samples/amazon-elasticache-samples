# Los Angeles Santa Monica Store Note
**Store:** LAX-003 (Santa Monica — 3rd Street Promenade)
**Region:** US-CA
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Layout
- Open-air entrance on 3rd Street Promenade (pedestrian-only). Vehicle access via Arizona Ave loading dock.
- Two floors: ground (footwear, accessories, pickup desk), upper (apparel, fitting rooms).
- Curbside pickup: customer parks in Structure 4 (city garage on 4th St), texts arrival, associate walks order out to 2nd floor of garage. ~5 min process.

## Special Procedures
- **Beach traffic:** Summer weekends (Memorial Day–Labor Day) expect 3x volume. Extend curbside hours to 9 PM.
- **Influencer visits:** Store is frequently used for social media content. Photography allowed in-store but no tripods/lighting rigs without manager approval and liability waiver.
- **Homeless outreach:** If individuals are sleeping near entrance, contact Santa Monica PD non-emergency line (not store security). Do not confront directly.

## Parking
- No store lot. Structure 4 (4th & Arizona): first 90 min free, then $2/hr. Validate with any purchase for additional 1 hour free.
