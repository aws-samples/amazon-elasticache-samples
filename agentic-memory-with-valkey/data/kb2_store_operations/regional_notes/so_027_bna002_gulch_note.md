# Nashville Gulch Store Note
**Store:** BNA-002 (The Gulch)
**Region:** US-TN
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Street-level in The Gulch neighborhood. ~2,500 sq ft.
- Curbside: 2 dedicated spots on 11th Ave S. Customer texts arrival.
- Popular with bachelorette party groups (Nashville is #1 bachelorette destination).

## Special Procedures
- **Bachelorette groups:** Groups of 6+ are common, especially Fri–Sun. They browse extensively but conversion is high on accessories (hats, socks, fun items). Be patient, don't rush.
- **CMA Fest (Jun):** Country Music Association festival. Broadway is 3 blocks away. Expect 2x traffic Thu–Sun. Stock cowboy-adjacent accessories if available.
- **Nashville SC / Titans game days:** Geodis Park and Nissan Stadium are nearby. Pre-game foot traffic spikes 2–3 hours before kickoff. Stock team-neutral athletic wear.
- **Flash flooding:** The Gulch sits in a low area. Monitor NWS alerts during heavy rain. If 11th Ave floods, suspend curbside immediately.

## Parking
- Small lot behind store: 8 spots, free for customers. Overflow: public garage on Pine St ($10 flat rate).
