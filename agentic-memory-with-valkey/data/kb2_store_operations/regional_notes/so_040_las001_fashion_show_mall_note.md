# Las Vegas Fashion Show Mall Store Note
**Store:** LAS-001 (Fashion Show Mall)
**Region:** US-NV
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Major Strip-adjacent mall on Las Vegas Blvd. ~3,300 sq ft.
- Pickup inside store. No curbside (mall policy + Strip traffic makes it impossible).
- Tourist-heavy: 80%+ of customers are visitors, not locals.

## Special Procedures
- **Convention traffic:** CES (Jan), MAGIC (Feb/Aug), and other major conventions drive massive foot traffic. Check Vegas convention calendar monthly and staff accordingly.
- **Late hours:** Mall hours are 10 AM–9 PM, but Strip foot traffic peaks 8–11 PM. Cannot extend past mall hours.
- **Cash transactions:** Higher-than-average cash transactions from casino visitors. Ensure adequate cash drawer float ($500 minimum). Armored car pickup Mon/Thu.
- **Returns from impulse buys:** Tourist impulse buy return rate is ~25% (vs 12% national average). Many returns happen at other stores nationwide. This is expected — do not flag as suspicious.
- **No sales tax on clothing in Nevada:** Wait — Nevada DOES have sales tax (8.375% in Clark County). Do NOT tell customers there's no tax. Common misconception from Montana/Oregon visitors.

## Parking
- Fashion Show Mall garage: free. Massive. Direct to Level 2 near Nordstrom.
