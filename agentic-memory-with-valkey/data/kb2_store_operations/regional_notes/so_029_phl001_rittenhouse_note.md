# Philadelphia Rittenhouse Store Note
**Store:** PHL-001 (Rittenhouse Square)
**Region:** US-PA
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Street-level on Walnut St near Rittenhouse Square. ~2,300 sq ft.
- Pickup at register. No curbside — Walnut St is too congested.
- Strong running community — close to Schuylkill River Trail.

## Special Procedures
- **Philadelphia Marathon (Nov):** Route passes through Center City. Pre-stage race essentials 2 weeks prior. Extended hours race weekend.
- **Broad Street Run (May):** 10-mile race, 40K runners. Second-largest road race in US. Stock accordingly.
- **Eagles game days (Sep–Jan):** If Eagles are playing at home (Lincoln Financial Field, 5 miles south), expect lower foot traffic during game time, spike after. Adjust staffing.
- **Snow/ice:** Philadelphia gets real winters. Follow Newbury St (BOS-001) winter protocol for sidewalk clearing. City fines for unshoveled sidewalks.

## Parking
- Street metered parking. Nearest garage: Rittenhouse Garage (Moravian St), $8/hr. No validation.
