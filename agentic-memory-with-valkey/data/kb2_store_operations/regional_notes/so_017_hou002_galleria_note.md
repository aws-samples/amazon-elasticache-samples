# Houston Galleria Store Note
**Store:** HOU-002 (The Galleria)
**Region:** US-TX
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Mall location, Level 1 near Nordstrom wing. ~4,000 sq ft.
- Pickup desk inside store, left of entrance.
- No curbside (mall policy). Customers pick up in-store only.

## Special Procedures
- **Hurricane season (Jun–Nov):** If NWS issues hurricane watch for Harris County, begin pre-closure protocol: secure displays, back up POS, notify pending pickup customers. Store Manager has authority to close without Regional Ops approval during active warnings.
- **Flooding protocol:** Galleria area floods during heavy rain. If Westheimer Rd or Post Oak Blvd are impassable, activate IVR message and suppress store from online pickup. Do NOT wait for mall to officially close.
- **Summer heat:** Curbside would be available but mall prohibits it. If customer calls requesting car delivery due to heat/disability, offer to meet at mall valet stand (Level 1 entrance near Saks).

## Parking
- Galleria garage: free, massive. Direct customers to Level 1 Red zone near Nordstrom for closest walk.
