# San Francisco Union Square Store Note
**Store:** SFO-001 (Union Square)
**Region:** US-CA
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Two-floor retail on Post St near Union Square. ~3,400 sq ft.
- Pickup desk on ground floor. No curbside — downtown congestion makes it impractical.
- Cable car stop (Powell-Mason line) is 1 block away — tourist foot traffic is constant.

## Special Procedures
- **Fog/cold summers:** San Francisco summers are cold. Stock lightweight layers and jackets year-round, not just seasonal. "Coldest winter I ever spent was a summer in San Francisco" is real.
- **Fleet Week (Oct):** Blue Angels air shows draw massive crowds to waterfront. Moderate spillover traffic to Union Square. Extended hours Sat–Sun of Fleet Week.
- **Retail theft prevention:** Union Square has elevated retail theft risk. All items over $100 secured with cable locks. Fitting room limit: 5 items. Associate must count items in/out. Security guard on duty during all open hours.
- **Earthquake preparedness:** Quarterly earthquake drill. Secure all heavy displays to walls. Emergency kit in stockroom: water, first aid, flashlights, radio.

## Parking
- Union Square Garage (under the square): $2.50/hr first 2 hours. No validation.
