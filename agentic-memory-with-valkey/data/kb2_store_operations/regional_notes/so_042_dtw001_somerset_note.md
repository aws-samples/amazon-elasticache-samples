## Store Details
- Upscale mall in Troy, MI (suburb of Detroit). ~2,800 sq ft, South building Level 1.
- Pickup inside store. No curbside (mall policy).
- Customer base skews higher income — stock premium products prominently.

## Special Procedures
- **Auto show (Jan):** North American International Auto Show in downtown Detroit. Moderate traffic increase from out-of-town visitors.
- **Winter driving:** Michigan winters are harsh. Customer no-shows for pickup spike during snow events. Extend hold timers by 24 hours automatically Nov–Mar.
- **Detroit Marathon (Oct):** Route is downtown, 20 miles from Somerset. Minimal direct impact but stock race essentials for local runners in the weeks prior.
- **Big Three employee discount:** Ford, GM, Stellantis employees frequently ask about corporate discounts. We do NOT have a Big Three corporate program. Politely redirect to standard promotions.
