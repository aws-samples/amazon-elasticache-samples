# Phoenix Scottsdale Quarter Store Note
**Store:** PHX-004 (Scottsdale Quarter)
**Region:** US-AZ
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Outdoor lifestyle center on N Scottsdale Rd. ~3,200 sq ft.
- Pickup desk at front counter. Curbside: 2 spots in Row A of parking lot, marked with orange cones.

## Special Procedures
- **Extreme heat (Jun–Sep):** When NWS issues excessive heat warning (115°F+), do NOT send associates to parking lot for curbside. Customer must come inside. Update app messaging: "For safety, curbside is paused during extreme heat. Please pick up inside our air-conditioned store."
- **Snowbird season (Nov–Mar):** 40% traffic increase from seasonal residents. Stock extra wide sizes and walking shoes. Schedule additional fitting associate.
- **Golf tournament week (Feb):** WM Phoenix Open drives tourism. Extend hours to 9 PM, stock extra accessories and sun protection gear.

## Parking
- Open-air lot, free. Covered spots in Row A fill fast — direct overflow to Row D (shaded by building in afternoon).
