# Seattle Downtown Flagship Curbside Capacity Note
**Store:** SEA-001 (Seattle Downtown Flagship)  
**Region:** US-WA  
**Status:** Active  
**Effective:** 2026-02-20 to 2026-03-15

## Summary
SEA-001 is experiencing reduced curbside capacity due to loading zone construction near the main pickup entrance.

## Operational Impact
- Curbside remains available, but wait times may be longer than normal
- Peak period delays expected: 4:00 PM to 7:30 PM local time
- Large-item curbside handoff may be temporarily paused during congestion windows

## Agent Guidance
- Set expectations for longer curbside wait times
- Offer in-store pickup as faster option when customer is able to enter store
- Do not promise curbside handoff within a fixed number of minutes

## Store Associate Guidance
- Prioritize time-sensitive orders and customers with accessibility needs
- Use alternate loading zone B when available
- If curbside queue exceeds local safety threshold, temporarily switch new arrivals to in-store pickup instructions

## Notes
This note overrides baseline curbside timing expectations in the US Pickup & Curbside Operations Playbook for SEA-001 during the effective period.
