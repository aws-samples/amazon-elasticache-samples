# Minneapolis Mall of America Store Note
**Store:** MSP-001 (Mall of America)
**Region:** US-MN
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Level 2, East wing near Nickelodeon Universe. ~3,600 sq ft.
- Pickup inside store. No curbside (mall policy).
- Mall is a major tourist destination — expect out-of-state and international customers year-round.

## Special Procedures
- **No sales tax on clothing in Minnesota.** This is a major draw for out-of-state shoppers. Train staff to mention this proactively. Signage at entrance: "No sales tax on clothing & shoes in MN!"
- **Polar vortex (Dec–Feb):** When wind chill drops below -30°F, mall becomes a refuge. Expect 50% traffic increase on extreme cold days as people seek indoor activities.
- **Mall of America events:** Frequent concerts, celebrity appearances at rotunda. Check mall event calendar weekly and staff accordingly.
- **Light rail access:** Blue Line stops at Mall of America station. Many customers arrive without cars — offer to hold large purchases for next-day pickup if customer can't carry.

## Parking
- MOA garage: free, 12,000+ spots. Direct to East lot, Level 2 for closest access.
