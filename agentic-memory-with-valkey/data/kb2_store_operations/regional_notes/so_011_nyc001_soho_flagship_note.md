# Manhattan SoHo Flagship Store Note
**Store:** NYC-001 (SoHo Flagship)
**Region:** US-NY
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Layout
- Ground floor: footwear + accessories. Second floor: apparel + fitting rooms. Basement: stockroom only (no customer access).
- Pickup desk located at rear of ground floor near freight elevator.
- Curbside not available — no dedicated loading zone on Broadway. All pickups are in-store only.

## Special Procedures
- **Tourist traffic:** Weekends and holidays expect 2x normal foot traffic. Staff pickup desk with minimum 2 associates Fri–Sun.
- **Theft prevention:** All footwear displays are single-shoe. Second shoe retrieved from stockroom by associate. High-value items ($150+) require manager unlock from display case.
- **Delivery acceptance:** Vendor deliveries only accepted 6–9 AM via basement freight entrance on Crosby St. No exceptions during store hours.

## Parking / Access
- No store parking. Nearest garage: 100 Broadway Garage (2 blocks south). Customer can validate for 1 hour with $75+ purchase.
- Subway: N/R/W to Prince St, 6 to Spring St.
