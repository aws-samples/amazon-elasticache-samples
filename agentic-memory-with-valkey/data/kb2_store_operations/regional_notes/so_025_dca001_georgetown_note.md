# Washington DC Georgetown Store Note
**Store:** DCA-001 (Georgetown)
**Region:** US-DC
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Street-level retail on M St NW in Georgetown. ~2,800 sq ft, single floor.
- Pickup at front register. Curbside: 1 metered spot on M St — unreliable. Recommend in-store pickup.
- No Metro station in Georgetown. Customers arrive by bus, car, or walk from Foggy Bottom station (15 min).

## Special Procedures
- **Cherry Blossom Festival (late Mar–early Apr):** 1.5M visitors to DC. Expect 2x traffic. Stock running gear for National Cherry Blossom 10-Miler participants.
- **Marine Corps Marathon (Oct):** Route passes through Georgetown. Road closures affect deliveries Sat–Sun. Schedule vendor deliveries for Thursday/Friday of race week.
- **Government shutdown impact:** During federal shutdowns, Georgetown foot traffic drops ~20% (fewer federal workers in area). Reduce staffing accordingly.
- **Embassy Row proximity:** International customers common. Accept international credit cards. Staff with language diversity when possible.

## Parking
- Street metered parking (2-hour limit). Nearest garage: Georgetown Park (M St & Wisconsin Ave), $5/hr.
