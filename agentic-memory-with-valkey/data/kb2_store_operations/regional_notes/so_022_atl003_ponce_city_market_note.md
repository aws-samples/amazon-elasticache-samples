# Atlanta Ponce City Market Store Note
**Store:** ATL-003 (Ponce City Market)
**Region:** US-GA
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Mixed-use development on the BeltLine. ~2,600 sq ft, ground floor.
- Pickup at register. Curbside: customer parks on N Ave side, texts arrival, associate walks out. ~3 min.
- BeltLine foot traffic is significant — store gets walk-ins from trail users.

## Special Procedures
- **BeltLine events:** Lantern Parade (Sep), BeltLine running events — expect 3x walk-in traffic. Pre-stage running socks, hydration, and impulse accessories near entrance.
- **Peachtree Road Race (Jul 4):** World's largest 10K. Stock race-day essentials (socks, body glide, hydration) 2 weeks prior. Extended hours Jul 3: open at 7 AM.
- **Summer heat/humidity:** Georgia summers are brutal. Keep water cooler stocked near entrance for walk-in trail users. Good will gesture, drives foot traffic.

## Parking
- Ponce City Market garage: first 1 hour free, then $3/hr. Validate for additional 1 hour with purchase.
