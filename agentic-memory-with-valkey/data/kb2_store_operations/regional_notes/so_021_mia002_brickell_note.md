# Miami Brickell City Centre Store Note
**Store:** MIA-002 (Brickell City Centre)
**Region:** US-FL
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Open-air mall, Level 2 near climate ribbon walkway. ~3,000 sq ft.
- Pickup at front counter. No curbside (mall structure doesn't support it).
- Climate ribbon provides shade/rain protection for walkways but store entrance is exposed during heavy rain.

## Special Procedures
- **Hurricane season (Jun–Nov):** Same protocol as HOU-002. Store Manager can close without Regional Ops approval during active NWS warnings. Pre-stage hurricane prep kit (tarps, tape, battery backup for POS).
- **Cruise ship days:** Port of Miami is 2 miles east. Cruise embarkation days (typically Sat/Sun) bring tourist traffic. Staff bilingual associate (Spanish/English) on weekends.
- **Rain protocol:** Afternoon thunderstorms are daily Jun–Sep. Place wet floor signs at entrance. Keep umbrella bags stocked at door.
- **Art Basel week (Dec):** Major international art fair. Expect 2x traffic from Wynwood/Design District overflow. Extended hours: 10 AM–10 PM.

## Parking
- Brickell City Centre garage: first 3 hours free with validation. Validate at any register.
