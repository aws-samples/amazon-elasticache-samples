# Boston Newbury Street Store Note
**Store:** BOS-001 (Newbury Street)
**Region:** US-MA
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Street-level brownstone retail on Newbury St between Dartmouth and Exeter. ~2,100 sq ft. Two levels: ground (footwear, pickup) and lower (apparel).
- No curbside — Newbury St has no loading zones during business hours. All pickups in-store.
- Narrow staircase to lower level — not ADA accessible. Bring apparel items upstairs for customers who cannot navigate stairs.

## Special Procedures
- **Boston Marathon (Apr, Patriots' Day):** Route passes within 2 blocks. Expect massive foot traffic Sat–Mon. Pre-stage marathon essentials. Extended hours marathon Monday: 7 AM–9 PM.
- **Nor'easter protocol:** If NWS issues blizzard warning, follow emergency closure playbook (so_010). Post-storm: shovel sidewalk before opening. City fines for unshoveled sidewalks within 3 hours of snowfall ending.
- **College move-in (Aug/Sep):** BU, Northeastern, Berklee students flood Back Bay. Stock entry-level running shoes and training gear. Student discount applies (15% with .edu ID).
- **Winter salt damage:** Hardwood floors in store. Increase mop rotation to every hour Nov–Mar. Place boot brush mat at entrance.

## Parking
- No store parking. Nearest: Prudential Center garage (3 blocks). No validation offered.
