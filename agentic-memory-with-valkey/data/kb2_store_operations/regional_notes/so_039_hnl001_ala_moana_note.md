# Honolulu Ala Moana Store Note
**Store:** HNL-001 (Ala Moana Center)
**Region:** US-HI
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Open-air mall, Level 2 near Bloomingdale's. ~2,700 sq ft.
- Pickup at front counter. No curbside (mall policy).
- Heavy tourist traffic — Japanese, Korean, and Australian visitors are significant customer segments.

## Special Procedures
- **International customers:** Accept international credit cards. Tax-free shopping forms available for international visitors (Hawaii has GET, not traditional sales tax — explain if asked).
- **Language support:** Japanese-speaking associate on staff Mon–Sat. Korean-speaking associate Thu–Sun.
- **Shipping to mainland:** Customers frequently ask about shipping purchases home. Offer UPS shipping from store ($12 flat rate for orders under 5 lbs, actual cost above). Packing materials in stockroom.
- **Lei greeting:** For grand opening events or VIP visits, coordinate lei delivery with Cindy's Lei Shoppe (vendor on file). 24-hour advance notice required.
- **Humidity:** Store humidity is high year-round. Rotate shoe box stock to prevent mold. Check weekly.
