# Austin Domain Store Note
**Store:** AUS-001 (The Domain)
**Region:** US-TX
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Outdoor lifestyle center in North Austin. ~3,100 sq ft.
- Curbside: 3 spots in front of store on Domain Dr. Well-marked.
- Strong tech worker customer base (Apple, Meta, Google, Amazon offices nearby).

## Special Procedures
- **SXSW (Mar):** South by Southwest brings 300K+ visitors. Downtown stores get hammered; Domain gets moderate spillover. Stock extra casual/athleisure. Extended hours during SXSW week.
- **ACL Festival (Oct):** Austin City Limits in Zilker Park. Similar to SXSW — moderate Domain impact. Stock sun protection and comfortable shoes.
- **Heat protocol (Jun–Sep):** Triple-digit heat common. Curbside associates limited to 10-min outdoor shifts. Rotate frequently. Keep cooler with water bottles at curbside station.
- **Tech company bulk orders:** Occasionally get corporate wellness program orders (10+ pairs). Route to store manager for bulk processing. Cannot hold inventory for unconfirmed bulk orders.

## Parking
- Domain surface lots and garage: free. Ample parking except during holiday season.
