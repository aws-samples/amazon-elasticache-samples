# San Diego UTC Store Note
**Store:** SAN-003 (Westfield UTC)
**Region:** US-CA
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Open-air mall in La Jolla / University City area. ~2,900 sq ft.
- Pickup at front counter. Curbside: customer parks in designated pickup spots near Vons entrance, texts arrival.
- UCSD campus is 1 mile away — significant student traffic.

## Special Procedures
- **Year-round mild weather:** San Diego doesn't have seasonal extremes. Stock is consistent year-round. Slight uptick in trail shoes spring/fall (hiking season).
- **Comic-Con (Jul):** 130K attendees in downtown SD. UTC gets moderate spillover from tourists. Stock fun/colorful accessories.
- **Military discount:** Large military population (Camp Pendleton, Naval Base SD, Miramar). Honor 15% military discount with valid DoD ID. Train all staff on acceptable ID forms.
- **UCSD finals weeks:** Foot traffic drops during finals (mid-Mar, mid-Jun, mid-Dec). Reduce staffing.

## Parking
- UTC open-air lot and garage: free. Ample parking.
