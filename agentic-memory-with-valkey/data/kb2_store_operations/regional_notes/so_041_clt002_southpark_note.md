# Charlotte SouthPark Mall Store Note
**Store:** CLT-002 (SouthPark Mall)
**Region:** US-NC
**Status:** Active
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Store Details
- Mall location, Level 1 near Nordstrom. ~3,000 sq ft.
- Pickup inside store. No curbside (mall policy).
- Strong banking sector customer base (Bank of America HQ, Wells Fargo, Truist all nearby).

## Special Procedures
- **NASCAR race weekends (May, Oct):** Charlotte Motor Speedway events bring 100K+ visitors. Moderate SouthPark impact. Stock casual athletic wear.
- **Banking hours traffic:** Weekday lunch hour (11:30 AM–1:30 PM) is peak — office workers from nearby towers. Staff accordingly.
- **Hurricane season:** Charlotte is inland but gets remnants. Follow standard weather protocol. Rarely requires closure but monitor for flooding on Fairview Rd (store access road).
- **Panthers/Hornets game days:** Bank of America Stadium and Spectrum Center are uptown (6 miles north). Minimal SouthPark impact.
