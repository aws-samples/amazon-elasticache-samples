# Store Safety & Emergency Closure Playbook (US)

## Purpose
Procedures for temporary store closures due to weather, safety incidents, or infrastructure failures. Applies to all US stores.

## Closure Authorization
- **Planned closure** (construction, fumigation): Regional Ops Manager, 72-hour advance notice required.
- **Emergency closure** (weather, power, safety): Store Manager can close immediately; notify Regional Ops within 1 hour.

## Customer Communication
- Update store hours on website/app within 30 minutes of closure decision.
- Pending pickup orders: auto-notify customers with "Your pickup at [Store] is temporarily unavailable. We'll notify you when the store reopens, or you can choose free shipping instead."
- Phone queue: activate IVR closure message within 15 minutes.

## Order Handling During Closure
- All pending pickups: extend hold timer by duration of closure (no auto-cancel during closure).
- Ship-from-store orders: reroute to nearest eligible store via StoreLink.
- New online orders: suppress store from pickup options until reopened.

## Reopening Checklist
1. Safety walkthrough by Store Manager
2. Re-enable store in pickup/ship-from-store systems
3. Process backlogged pickups (oldest first)
4. Update website/app hours
5. Deactivate IVR closure message
