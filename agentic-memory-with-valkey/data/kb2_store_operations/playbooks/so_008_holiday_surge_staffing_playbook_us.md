# Holiday Surge Staffing Playbook (US)

## Purpose
Standard operating procedures for scaling store pickup and curbside staffing during peak holiday periods (Black Friday through Jan 2). Applies to all US stores unless overridden by a regional note.

## Staffing Tiers
| Daily Order Volume | Min Curbside Staff | Min Pickup Desk Staff |
|---|---|---|
| < 100 | 2 | 1 |
| 100–250 | 4 | 2 |
| 250–500 | 6 | 3 |
| 500+ | 8 + overflow | 4 |

## Overflow Protocol
- When queue exceeds 15-minute wait: activate overflow lane (if available) or redirect to next-day pickup with $10 credit.
- Overflow staff pulled from fitting room / floor coverage — never from returns desk.

## Communication Templates
- **Customer wait notification:** "Your order at [Store] is ready! Current estimated wait for curbside: [X] minutes. Need to reschedule? Reply LATER for next-day pickup with a $10 credit."
- **Escalation to store manager:** Trigger when wait exceeds 25 minutes for 3+ consecutive customers.

## Hours Extension
Stores in Tier 1 metros (NYC, LA, Chicago, Houston, Phoenix, Dallas) extend curbside to 10 PM during surge. All others follow standard 8 PM cutoff.

## Post-Holiday Wind-Down
- Jan 3–7: Maintain 75% surge staffing for returns wave.
- Jan 8+: Return to standard levels unless regional note specifies otherwise.
