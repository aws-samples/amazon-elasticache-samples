# Approved Response Templates: Product Safety Incident Follow-Up
**Template Version:** LEG-SAFE-FOLLOWUP-US-v1.1
**Status:** Approved (Internal Use Only)
**Effective:** 2026-01-15

## Purpose
Legal-approved follow-up language after a product safety incident has been reported and initially handled per pc_010 (intake) and pc_017 (response policy).

## Template 1: Follow-Up After Customer Shipped Product for Inspection
> "Thank you for sending the [product name] to our safety team. We've received it and our engineers are conducting a thorough inspection. We expect to have findings within [10–15] business days. In the meantime, your replacement has been shipped and should arrive by [date]. We'll follow up with you once the inspection is complete."

## Template 2: Inspection Complete — Manufacturing Defect Confirmed
> "Our safety team has completed the inspection of your [product name]. We identified a manufacturing issue that caused the [describe symptom — e.g., overheating]. This issue has been documented and shared with our engineering team to prevent it in future production. Your replacement unit does not have this issue. Thank you again for reporting this — it helps us improve our products for all customers."

## Template 3: Inspection Complete — No Defect Found
> "Our safety team has completed the inspection of your [product name]. Based on our testing, the device is functioning within normal parameters. The [symptom] you experienced may have been caused by [environmental factor / third-party accessory / one-time event]. If you experience the issue again with your replacement unit, please don't hesitate to contact us."

## Template 4: Inspection Complete — Counterfeit Product Confirmed
> "Our safety team has completed the inspection and determined that the product you received was not manufactured by Accent Athletics. This means it did not go through our quality and safety testing processes. We strongly recommend not using any similar products from the same seller. Your full refund has been processed, and we're here to help you find genuine Accent Athletics products."

## Template 5: CPSC Report Filed — Customer Notification (Use Only When Required by Law)
> "As part of our commitment to product safety, we've reported this incident to the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) as required by federal law. This is a standard process and does not necessarily mean there is a widespread safety issue. The CPSC may contact you for additional information. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to reach out to us."

**Note:** Template 5 requires Legal team approval before sending. Do not send without explicit authorization from Legal.
