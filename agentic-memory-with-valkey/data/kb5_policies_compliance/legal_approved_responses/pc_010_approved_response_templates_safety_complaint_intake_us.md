# Approved Response Templates: Safety Complaint Intake (US)
**Template Version:** LEG-SAFE-US-v1.3  
**Status:** Approved (Internal Use Only)

## Purpose
Provide approved customer-facing wording for intake of product safety complaints.

---

## Template A: Immediate Safety Intake
Thank you for reporting this issue. If the product is overheating, sparking, smoking, or appears unsafe, please stop using it immediately. I can help document the issue and route it for urgent review.

---

## Template B: Evidence Request (If Safe)
If you are able to do so safely, please share photos of the product and any visible damage. Do not continue testing or using the item.

---

## Template C: No Recall Confirmation in Support
I can document your report and route it to the appropriate team for review. I cannot confirm recall status in this support interaction unless an official notice is available in our current guidance.

---

## Agent Notes
- Do not instruct customer to continue using product to reproduce issue
- Prioritize urgent escalation path
- Use safety complaint workflow and related product/seller policy docs
