# Approved Response Templates: Privacy Requests (US)
**Template Version:** LEG-PRIV-US-v1.9  
**Status:** Approved (Internal Use Only)

## Purpose
Provide legally approved customer-facing wording for common privacy request intake scenarios. Agents may personalize factual details but should not alter required legal statements.

---

## Template A: Privacy Request Acknowledgment (General)
Thank you for contacting us regarding your privacy request. We can help route your request to the appropriate team. To protect your information, we may need to verify certain details before processing your request. Once submitted, you will receive updates based on the request type and applicable process.

---

## Template B: Identity Verification Needed
Thank you for your request. Before we can process this request, we need additional information to verify that you are authorized to make the request. This verification step is required to protect customer information and prevent unauthorized access.

---

## Template C: Cannot Complete in Support Channel
Thank you for your message. This request requires handling through our privacy request process. We can provide the correct submission path and general guidance, but we cannot complete the request directly in this support channel.

---

## Agent Notes
- Do not promise deletion, correction, or access completion during first contact
- Do not interpret legal eligibility rules on the fly
- Use privacy workflow and escalation process for unusual or disputed requests
