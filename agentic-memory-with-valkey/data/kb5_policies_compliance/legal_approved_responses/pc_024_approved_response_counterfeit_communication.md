# Approved Response Templates: Counterfeit Product Customer Communication
**Template Version:** LEG-COUNTERFEIT-US-v1.0
**Status:** Approved (Internal Use Only)
**Effective:** 2026-02-15

## Purpose
Legal-approved language for communicating with customers who purchased counterfeit products from suspended sellers. Currently applicable to PeakForm Athletics (PFA-US-720).

## Template 1: Initial Contact — Customer Doesn't Know Product Is Counterfeit
> "Thank you for contacting Accent Athletics. I've looked into your order, and I want to let you know that the seller you purchased from has been removed from our marketplace because some of their products did not meet our authenticity standards. We want to make this right for you — I can process a full refund for your order right now. Would you like me to do that?"

## Template 2: Customer Asks "Is My Product Fake?"
> "We've identified that some products sold by this seller did not meet Accent Athletics' authenticity and quality standards. While we can't confirm the status of every individual item, we're offering a full refund to all affected customers as a precaution. I'd recommend discontinuing use of the product, and I'm happy to help you find the genuine version if you're interested."

## Template 3: Customer Reports Safety Issue with Counterfeit Product
> "I'm glad you're safe, and thank you for reporting this. Because this product was not manufactured by Accent Athletics, it may not meet our safety standards. Please discontinue use of the device immediately. I'm processing a full refund for you now. Our safety team would also like to inspect the product — would you be willing to ship it to us? We'll provide a prepaid label and a $50 account credit for your help."

## Template 4: Customer Demands Compensation Beyond Refund
> "I completely understand your frustration. In addition to the full refund, I'd like to offer you a [$X] account credit as a gesture of goodwill. If you'd like to purchase the genuine version of the product, I can also help you find it on our website or through one of our authorized sellers."

**Credit limits:** Per pc_021 exception policy. Tier 1: up to $25. Tier 2: up to $75.

## Words to AVOID
| Don't Say | Say Instead |
|---|---|
| Counterfeit | Did not meet authenticity standards |
| Fake | Not manufactured by Accent Athletics |
| Knockoff | Did not meet our quality standards |
| Banned (seller) | Removed from our marketplace |
| Illegal | (don't reference legality at all) |
| Lawsuit / legal action | (never reference legal proceedings) |
