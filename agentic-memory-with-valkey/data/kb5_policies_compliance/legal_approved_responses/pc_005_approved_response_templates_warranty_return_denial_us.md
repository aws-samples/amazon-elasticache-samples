# Approved Response Templates: Warranty and Return Denial Messaging (US)
**Template Version:** LEG-RET-US-v2.1  
**Status:** Approved (Internal Use Only)

## Purpose
Provide approved wording for return ineligibility, seller-policy differences, and warranty denial or limitation messaging.

---

## Template A: Return Outside Policy Window (Standard)
I understand the request. Based on the order details available, this item appears to be outside the applicable return window. If you believe there are additional circumstances, I can help review the order and route it for further review where appropriate.

---

## Template B: Seller-Fulfilled Marketplace Policy Difference
Because this item was sold and fulfilled by a marketplace seller, the return process and eligibility may differ from retailer-fulfilled items. I can help confirm the seller policy and available marketplace protections for your order.

---

## Template C: Warranty Coverage Not Confirmed Yet
I understand your concern. Based on the information currently available, I cannot confirm warranty coverage in this support step. I can help document the issue and route the case for warranty review under the applicable policy.

---

## Template D: Defect Claim Needs More Information
Thank you for reporting the issue. To review this claim under the applicable policy, we may need additional details such as photos, issue description, and when the problem started.

---

## Agent Notes
- Avoid absolute statements like "You are not covered" unless final determination is documented
- Use neutral wording
- Follow seller/marketplace ownership rules before quoting return/warranty policy
