# Severe Weather Delivery Delay Exception (US-NW)
**Exception ID:** TEMP-SHIP-USNW-WX-2026-02  
**Region:** US-NW  
**Status:** Active  
**Effective:** 2026-02-21 through 2026-02-28

## Purpose
Temporary policy guidance for customer communications and support handling during severe weather-related delivery delays in the US Northwest region.

## Scope
Applies to eligible shipments to affected US-NW destinations experiencing carrier disruptions during the effective period.

## Temporary Guidance
- Delivery estimates may exceed standard shipping windows without immediate lost-package escalation
- Agents should reference tracking first and acknowledge regional carrier disruption
- Do not guarantee revised delivery date unless carrier tracking provides one

## Customer Support Guidance
- Use empathetic language and acknowledge weather disruption
- Offer cancellation options only if order status and policy allow
- Do not classify delayed package as lost before temporary exception threshold is met

## Override Effect
This exception temporarily modifies delivery delay handling expectations in the US Shipping and Delivery Policy (SHIP-US-v3.2) for in-scope orders and dates only.

## Expiration Handling
After the effective end date, revert to baseline shipping policy unless a renewed exception is published.
