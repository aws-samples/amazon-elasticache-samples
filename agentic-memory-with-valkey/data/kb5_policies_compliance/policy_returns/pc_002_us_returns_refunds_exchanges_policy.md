# US Returns, Refunds, and Exchanges Policy
**Policy Version:** RET-US-v4.1  
**Region:** US  
**Status:** Approved

## Purpose
Define eligibility, timing, and conditions for returns, refunds, and exchanges for US purchases.

## Standard Return Window
Most eligible items may be returned within **30 calendar days** of delivery or in-store purchase date.

## Refund Timing
After return is received and processed:
- Refunds to original payment method are typically issued within 3 to 10 business days
- Bank/card posting times may vary

## Exchange Eligibility
Exchanges are supported for eligible items where exchange workflow is available. If exchange workflow is unavailable, agents may guide customer through return + repurchase process.

## Item Condition Requirements
Eligible returns generally require items to be:
- In original condition
- Not excessively worn/used (unless defect claim applies)
- Returned with original components/accessories when required

## Non-Returnable / Restricted Returns (Examples)
The following may be non-returnable or restricted unless defective or required by law:
- Final sale items
- Certain hygiene-sensitive products once opened
- Personalized/customized items
- Hazardous materials where return transport is restricted

## Defective / Damaged / Not-as-Described
If customer reports defect, shipping damage, or item not as described, use defect/damage claim handling flow rather than standard remorse return flow.

## Marketplace Orders
Marketplace seller-fulfilled orders may follow seller-specific return rules, subject to marketplace protections and applicable law. Check marketplace policy and seller-specific guidance before quoting return eligibility.

## Policy Overrides
If a temporary policy exception is active (for example, holiday return extension or weather disruption handling), the temporary exception takes precedence for eligible orders in scope.

## Agent Guidance
- Confirm purchase channel and fulfillment ownership before quoting return instructions
- Do not promise refund timing beyond policy ranges
- Use approved legal/compliance response templates for disputes or denied returns
