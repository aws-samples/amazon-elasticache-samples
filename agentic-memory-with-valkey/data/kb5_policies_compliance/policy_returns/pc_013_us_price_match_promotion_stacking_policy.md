# US Price Match and Promotion Stacking Policy
**Policy Version:** PRICE-US-v2.3
**Region:** US
**Status:** Approved
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Price Match Policy
Accent Athletics does NOT price match against:
- Marketplace sellers (RunReady, FitTech, TrailBound, GearVault, etc.)
- Other retailers (Amazon, REI, Dick's, etc.)
- International pricing
- Clearance, open-box, or renewed pricing (GearVault's lower prices reflect product condition, not a competitive price)

**Exception:** If accentathletics.com drops the price of an item within 14 days of a customer's direct purchase, the customer may request a one-time price adjustment (refund of difference). Does not apply to marketplace seller purchases.

## Promotion Stacking Rules
- Only ONE promotional code per order
- Promotional codes cannot be combined with:
  - Student discount (15% with .edu ID — see KB2 store notes for CHI-009, SAN-003)
  - Military discount (15% with DoD ID — see KB2: so_030 San Diego store note)
  - Employee discount
  - Loyalty upgrade discount (e.g., PulseFit Band 3→4 upgrade discount from KB3: ts_036 transcript)
- **Exception:** Free shipping promotions stack with one product discount code

## Marketplace Seller Pricing
- Authorized sellers set their own prices. Accent Athletics does not control seller pricing.
- If customer asks "why is it cheaper on [seller]?": "Our authorized sellers set their own prices independently."
- Do NOT reference MAP (Minimum Advertised Price) policy to customers. MAP enforcement is internal (see KB4: vs_030 — note: the old MAP process is deprecated, now handled by Brand Protection).
- If seller price seems suspiciously low (>40% below retail): may indicate counterfeit. Consult authenticity verification guide (KB4: vs_022). PeakForm's $89.99 "SwiftPace" (retail $249.99) was a red flag.

## Loyalty Program Credits
- Accent Athletics Rewards members earn 1 point per $1 spent
- 100 points = $5 credit
- Credits can be combined with one promotional code
- Credits cannot be applied to marketplace seller orders (seller-fulfilled)
- Credits CAN be applied to GearVault orders (GearVault is on Accent Athletics payment platform)
