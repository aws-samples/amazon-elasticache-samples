# US Electronics Warranty Policy
**Policy Version:** WAR-ELEC-US-v2.0
**Region:** US
**Status:** Approved
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Scope
Covers all Accent Athletics connected devices sold in the US:
- SwiftPace Running Watch (all variants)
- AeroTrack Pro Earbuds
- PulseFit Band 4 and PulseFit Band 4 Pro
- StrideScale Smart Body Composition Scale
- SleepSync Tracker
- PowerBase Charging Dock

## Warranty Duration
| Purchase Source | Warranty Period |
|---|---|
| Direct (accentathletics.com, Accent Athletics stores) | 1 year from purchase date |
| Authorized reseller (RunReady, FitTech, TrailBound) | 1 year from purchase date |
| GearVault Outlet (renewed/open-box) | 90 days GearVault warranty (NOT Accent Athletics warranty) |
| Unauthorized / suspended sellers (e.g., PeakForm) | No warranty |

## What's Covered
- Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
- Battery defects (capacity drops below 80% within warranty period)
- Firmware-caused hardware damage (e.g., SwiftPace bricking during update — see KB3: ts_022)
- Sensor failures (HR, GPS, bioimpedance, radar) not caused by physical damage

## What's NOT Covered
- Physical damage (drops, crushes, cracks)
- Water damage beyond rated depth (PulseFit Band: 50m, SwiftPace: 50m, AeroTrack: IPX4 splash only, StrideScale/SleepSync: not water resistant)
- Cosmetic wear (scratches, scuffs, band discoloration)
- Third-party accessory damage (e.g., FitTech bundled screen protector causing touch issues — see KB4: vs_026 — is NOT a warranty claim)
- Counterfeit products (see KB4: vs_015, vs_020)
- Normal battery degradation after warranty period

## Warranty Claim Process
1. Customer contacts Accent Athletics support (not the seller, unless GearVault)
2. Agent troubleshoots per KB3 guides. Many "defects" are software issues with fixes.
3. If hardware defect confirmed: initiate warranty replacement via support portal
4. Customer receives prepaid shipping label → ships defective unit → replacement ships within 3–5 business days
5. For SwiftPace firmware bricking (ts_022): expedited replacement, no troubleshooting required — known manufacturer defect
