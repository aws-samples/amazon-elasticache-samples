# US Footwear & Apparel Warranty Policy
**Policy Version:** WAR-FA-US-v3.0
**Region:** US
**Status:** Approved
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Scope
Covers all Accent Athletics footwear and apparel sold in the US (see KB1 for full product catalog).

## Warranty Duration
| Purchase Source | Warranty Period |
|---|---|
| Direct (accentathletics.com, Accent Athletics stores) | 1 year from purchase date |
| Authorized reseller (RunReady, TrailBound) | 1 year from purchase date |
| GearVault Outlet (renewed/open-box) | 90 days GearVault warranty |
| Unauthorized / suspended sellers | No warranty |

## What's Covered — Footwear
- Sole separation or delamination
- Stitching failure
- Midsole compression beyond normal wear (shoe "bottoms out" within warranty)
- Waterproofing failure on GTX models within normal use (see KB4: vs_028 for TrailCore Grip 5 GTX batch defect)
- Manufacturing defects in materials (e.g., eyelets pulling out, tongue detaching)

## What's Covered — Apparel
- Seam failure
- Zipper defects (ThermoShield Running Jacket, AllSeason Training Hoodie)
- Fabric delamination or pilling beyond normal wear within 6 months
- DWR (Durable Water Repellent) coating failure on ThermoShield Jacket within 6 months

## What's NOT Covered
- Normal wear and tear (outsole tread wear, fabric fading)
- Damage from improper care (machine washing shoes, dryer heat on technical fabrics)
- Sizing dissatisfaction (this is a return, not a warranty claim — see pc_002 returns policy)
- Cosmetic issues on open-box/renewed items from GearVault
- Products from unauthorized sellers (see KB4: vs_015 PeakForm counterfeits)

## Sizing vs. Defect: The Key Distinction
Agents frequently confuse sizing complaints with defect claims:
- "The shoe is too narrow" → **sizing issue** → return/exchange per pc_002 or seller policy (KB4: vs_017 RunReady, vs_021 TrailBound)
- "The shoe's toe box is separating from the upper" → **defect** → warranty claim
- "The shoe runs small" → **sizing issue** → consult fit guide (KB1: pk_005) and process exchange
- "The shoe's midsole collapsed after 2 months" → **defect** → warranty claim

## Claim Process
1. Customer contacts Accent Athletics support
2. Agent asks for photos of defect (required for footwear claims)
3. If defect confirmed from photos: initiate warranty replacement
4. If unclear from photos: customer ships product for inspection (prepaid label). Decision within 5 business days.
5. Replacement ships same model/size. If out of stock: consult substitution playbook (KB1: pk_006) for approved alternatives.
