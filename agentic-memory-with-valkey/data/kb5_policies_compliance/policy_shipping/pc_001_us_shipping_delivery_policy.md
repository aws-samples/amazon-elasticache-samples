# US Shipping and Delivery Policy
**Policy Version:** SHIP-US-v3.2  
**Region:** US  
**Status:** Approved

## Purpose
Describe shipping methods, estimated delivery windows, fees, and delivery exception handling for US orders.

## Shipping Methods (US)

### Standard Shipping
- Estimated delivery: 3 to 7 business days after shipment
- Delivery timing varies by destination, carrier, and weather conditions

### Expedited Shipping
- Estimated delivery: 2 to 3 business days after shipment
- Not available for all items or destinations

### Priority Shipping
- Estimated delivery: 1 to 2 business days after shipment
- May be unavailable for hazardous, oversized, or marketplace seller-fulfilled items

## Order Processing Time
Processing time occurs before shipment and may vary by item availability, payment verification, and fulfillment location. Estimated delivery windows do not include processing time unless explicitly stated at checkout.

## Delivery Delays
Delivery delays may occur due to:
- Severe weather
- Carrier operational constraints
- High-volume periods
- Address verification issues
- Marketplace seller handling time (seller-fulfilled orders)

## Agent Guidance
- Do not guarantee delivery dates unless order is marked with a guaranteed delivery commitment
- Use order tracking and current carrier status before quoting estimates
- Check active temporary policy exceptions for regional delays

## Marketplace Seller-Fulfilled Orders
Shipping timelines for seller-fulfilled marketplace orders may differ from retailer-fulfilled orders. Agents should reference marketplace seller policy and order-specific tracking details.

## Lost Package Escalation (Policy-Level)
If tracking shows no movement beyond the policy threshold for the shipping method, route case through carrier/lost-package workflow per customer service SOP.
