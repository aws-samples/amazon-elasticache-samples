# US Shipping Policy: Hazardous Materials and Lithium Battery Restrictions
**Policy Version:** SHIP-HAZ-US-v1.2
**Region:** US
**Status:** Approved
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31
**Supplements:** SHIP-US-v3.2 (pc_001)

## Purpose
Defines shipping restrictions for products containing lithium batteries, in compliance with DOT/PHMSA and IATA regulations.

## Affected Products
| Product | Battery Type | Wh Rating | Shipping Restriction |
|---|---|---|---|
| SwiftPace Running Watch | Li-ion, 380 mAh | 1.46 Wh | Ground + Air OK |
| AeroTrack Pro (earbuds + case) | Li-ion, 55 mAh (each bud) + 500 mAh (case) | 3.85 Wh total | Ground + Air OK |
| PulseFit Band 4 / 4 Pro | Li-ion, 250 mAh | 0.96 Wh | Ground + Air OK |
| SleepSync | No battery (USB-C powered) | N/A | No restriction |
| StrideScale | 4x AAA alkaline | N/A | No restriction |
| PowerBase Charging Dock | No battery | N/A | No restriction |
| PowerBand Resistance Set | No battery | N/A | No restriction |

## Key Rules
- All Accent Athletics lithium battery products are **under 100 Wh** — eligible for both ground and air shipping without special documentation.
- Products must ship with battery installed in device (not loose). Replacement batteries ship separately with UN3481 label.
- **Alaska and Hawaii:** Air shipping required (no ground option). All AA electronics are air-eligible — no restriction.
- **International (future):** If Accent Athletics expands to international shipping, lithium battery products require Shipper's Declaration for Dangerous Goods. Not currently applicable.

## Marketplace Seller Compliance
- Authorized sellers (RunReady, FitTech, TrailBound) must comply with the same lithium battery shipping regulations.
- FitTech (electronics seller) has been briefed on requirements. Their Memphis warehouse is DOT-compliant.
- GearVault ships renewed electronics — same rules apply. Their Reno warehouse is compliant.
- If a customer reports receiving a lithium battery product with damaged or swollen battery: this is a **safety issue**. Follow safety complaint intake process (pc_010) immediately.

## Counterfeit Electronics Shipping Risk
Products from suspended seller PeakForm (KB4: vs_015) contained unbranded lithium batteries with no safety certification. If a customer reports a PeakForm product overheating or swelling during shipping/charging:
1. Follow safety complaint intake (pc_010)
2. Advise customer to stop using the product immediately
3. Do NOT advise customer to ship the product back via air — uncertified lithium batteries are a shipping hazard
4. Escalate to Product Safety team for safe disposal instructions
