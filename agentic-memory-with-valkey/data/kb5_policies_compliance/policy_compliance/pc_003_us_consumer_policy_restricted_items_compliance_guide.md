# US Consumer Policy and Restricted Items Compliance Guide
**Policy Version:** COMP-US-v2.7  
**Region:** US  
**Status:** Approved (Internal)

## Purpose
Provide compliance-aligned handling guidance for restricted item categories, consumer rights escalation, and policy-sensitive customer interactions.

## Scope
Applies to support interactions involving:
- Restricted items
- Safety complaints
- Privacy/data rights requests
- Warranty disputes
- Policy escalation requests

## Restricted Item Handling (Examples)
Some items may have shipping, return, or sale restrictions due to law, carrier rules, or platform policy. Agents must:
- Confirm item category restrictions before advising shipment/return options
- Avoid suggesting prohibited return methods
- Use approved response templates when restriction limits options

## Consumer Rights and Escalation
If customer cites a legal right, regulatory agency, or statutory return/warranty requirement:
- Do not dispute legal interpretation
- Acknowledge concern
- Route to appropriate escalation path using legal/compliance workflow
- Use approved response templates where available

## Privacy / Data Requests
For privacy requests (access, deletion, correction, opt-out types as applicable):
- Verify request type and identity requirements per privacy workflow
- Use approved privacy response templates
- Do not confirm completion until privacy operations confirms action

## Safety Complaints
For complaints involving overheating, smoke, sparks, injury, or suspected hazard:
- Prioritize customer safety intake steps
- Capture product and order details
- Use approved safety complaint language
- Escalate using urgent safety path

## Channel / Ownership Check
For marketplace orders, some policy handling may require seller plus marketplace escalation. Reference marketplace policy override guide when applicable.
