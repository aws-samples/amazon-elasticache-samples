# Legacy Goodwill Credit Policy — $10 Maximum (Deprecated)
**Policy Version:** GW-US-v2.0
**Status:** Deprecated

> **Status: DEPRECATED** | Last updated: 2026-01-31
> **Superseded by:** Updated goodwill credit tiers (effective Feb 1, 2026)

## Original Content (Do Not Use)
Prior to Feb 2026, Tier 1 agents were limited to a **$10 maximum goodwill credit** per interaction, with Tier 2 approval required for anything above $10.

## Current Policy (Use This Instead)
Effective Feb 1, 2026, goodwill credit limits were updated:

| Agent Level | Standard Limit | With Active Exception |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | $15 per interaction | Up to exception limit (e.g., $50 for SleepSync apnea per pc_023) |
| Tier 2 | $50 per interaction | Up to exception limit (e.g., $200 for SleepSync per pc_023) |
| Customer Experience Manager | $200 per interaction | Unlimited with VP approval |

## Why Deprecated
The $10 limit was insufficient for the complexity of current issues. A customer whose SwiftPace watch bricked (ts_022) or who received false apnea alerts (ts_021) deserves more than $10. The updated tiers give agents appropriate authority to resolve issues without excessive escalation.

## Common Mistake
Agents trained on the old policy sometimes say "the most I can offer is $10" when the current limit is $15 (or higher with active exceptions). Check for active temporary policy exceptions before quoting credit limits.

## Active Exceptions (as of Feb 2026)
- SleepSync apnea alert: up to $50 Tier 1 / $200 Tier 2 (pc_023)
- SwiftPace bricking: $25 credit + free replacement (ts_022 + pc_020)
- AeroTrack battery drain: $15 credit at agent discretion (ts_019)
