# Marketplace Policy Override Reference (US)
**Policy Version:** COMP-MKT-US-v1.4  
**Status:** Approved (Internal)

## Purpose
Clarify when marketplace-specific policy and seller-specific rules modify or replace retailer standard policy messaging.

## Core Principle
Retailer standard policies do not automatically apply to seller-fulfilled marketplace orders.

## Agent Requirements
Before quoting policy for marketplace order:
1. Confirm whether order is retailer-fulfilled or seller-fulfilled
2. Check seller-specific policy/process guidance
3. Confirm applicable marketplace protections
4. Apply legal/compliance guidance if dispute escalates

## Common Error to Avoid
Do not promise in-store return eligibility for seller-fulfilled marketplace orders unless order metadata explicitly indicates eligibility.

## Escalation Guidance
If ownership or policy applicability is unclear, use marketplace escalation routing rather than making a policy determination in the chat/call.
