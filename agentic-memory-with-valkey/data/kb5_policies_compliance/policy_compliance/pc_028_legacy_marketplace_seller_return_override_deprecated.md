# Legacy Marketplace Seller Return Override — Seller Handles All Returns (Deprecated)
**Policy Version:** MKT-RET-US-v1.0
**Status:** Deprecated

> **Status: DEPRECATED** | Last updated: 2025-11-30
> **Superseded by:** COMP-MKT-US-v1.4 (pc_009) and individual seller policies (KB4)

## Original Content (Do Not Use)
Prior to Dec 2025, the marketplace policy was simple: "All marketplace seller orders follow the seller's return policy. Accent Athletics does not process returns for marketplace purchases."

This blanket policy has been replaced with a nuanced approach:
- **Returns for non-defective items:** Still handled by the seller (RunReady, FitTech, TrailBound, GearVault)
- **Warranty claims for defective items:** Now handled by Accent Athletics for authorized sellers (manufacturer warranty applies)
- **Counterfeit products from suspended sellers:** Handled by Accent Athletics (refund from marketplace trust fund)

## Why Deprecated
The old policy caused customer frustration when they had a genuine product defect but were told "contact the seller." Sellers like RunReady and FitTech are not equipped to diagnose firmware bugs or manufacturing defects — that's Accent Athletics' responsibility.

## Common Mistake
Agents sometimes still tell marketplace customers "you need to contact the seller for all issues." This is WRONG for:
- Firmware/software issues (always Accent Athletics — see KB4: vs_018)
- Manufacturing defects under warranty (always Accent Athletics — see pc_011, pc_012)
- Counterfeit products (always Accent Athletics — see pc_021)

The seller only handles: wrong item, shipping damage, sizing exchanges, and non-defective returns.
