# Marketplace Seller Counterfeit and Suspension Policy
**Policy Version:** MKT-AUTH-US-v1.1
**Region:** US
**Status:** Approved
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Purpose
Defines the policy framework for identifying, investigating, and acting on counterfeit products and seller misconduct on the Accent Athletics marketplace.

## Seller Tiers and Trust Levels
| Tier | Examples | Trust Level | Monitoring |
|---|---|---|---|
| Authorized Reseller | RunReady (RRA-US-415), FitTech (FTS-US-502), TrailBound (TBO-US-833) | High | Quarterly review |
| Authorized Liquidation | GearVault (GVO-US-618) | Medium | Monthly review |
| Standard Marketplace | StrideZone (SZD-US-101), FlexWear (FWC-US-204), ActiveTech (ATM-US-330) | Medium | Monthly review |
| Probation | (none currently) | Low | Weekly review |
| Suspended | PeakForm (PFA-US-720) | None | N/A — removed |

## Suspension Triggers
| Violation | Action |
|---|---|
| Confirmed counterfeit (1 instance) | Immediate suspension |
| Safety incident linked to seller product | Immediate suspension pending investigation |
| Order defect rate >5% for 3 consecutive months | Probation → suspension if not corrected |
| 3+ MAP violations in 12 months | Probation |
| Customer data mishandling | Immediate suspension |

## Post-Suspension Customer Handling
When a seller is suspended:
1. All active listings removed within 24 hours
2. Pending orders: if not yet shipped, auto-cancelled with full refund. If shipped, customer keeps product + gets refund.
3. Past orders (90 days): eligible for refund on request (see KB4: vs_020 for PeakForm process)
4. Past orders (>90 days): case-by-case review by Marketplace team

## Agent Instructions
- Do NOT tell customers a seller is "under investigation" — only confirm suspension after it's official
- Use approved language: "This seller has been removed from our marketplace" (not "banned," "kicked off," "caught selling fakes")
- For PeakForm specifically: follow vs_020 counterfeit handling playbook
- For authenticity concerns about active sellers: follow vs_022 verification guide

## Reinstatement
- Suspended sellers may apply for reinstatement after 12 months
- Requires: root cause analysis, corrective action plan, third-party audit of inventory
- PeakForm is NOT eligible for reinstatement (counterfeit + safety incident = permanent ban)
