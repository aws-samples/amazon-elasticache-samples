# US Product Safety Recall and Incident Response Policy
**Policy Version:** SAFE-US-v1.8
**Region:** US
**Status:** Approved
**Effective:** 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

## Purpose
Defines the process for handling product safety incidents, potential recalls, and CPSC (Consumer Product Safety Commission) reporting obligations.

## Reporting Thresholds
| Incident Type | Reporting Trigger | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Injury or property damage | Any single incident | Immediate CPSC report within 24 hours + Product Safety team escalation |
| Overheating / fire risk | Any single incident | Immediate CPSC report + product hold pending investigation |
| Pattern of complaints (no injury) | 3+ similar complaints in 30 days | Product Safety team review within 48 hours |
| Counterfeit product safety concern | Any single incident | Product Safety + Brand Protection + Legal |

## Current Active Safety Concerns

### SwiftPace Firmware Bricking (KB3: ts_022)
- **Classification:** Product defect, NOT safety hazard
- The bricking issue renders the watch non-functional but does not pose a fire, shock, or injury risk
- No CPSC report required
- Warranty replacement process in place

### PeakForm Counterfeit Electronics (KB4: vs_029)
- **Classification:** Potential safety hazard — uncertified lithium batteries
- One overheating incident reported (no injury)
- CPSC report filed Feb 12, 2026
- Product Safety team monitoring for additional incidents
- All PeakForm electronics customers being offered refunds (KB4: vs_020)

### ActiveTech USB Charger Overheating (KB4: vs_011)
- **Classification:** Under investigation
- Multiple overheating reports but no fire or injury
- ActiveTech has pulled affected SKUs pending investigation
- If customer reports ActiveTech charger overheating: follow safety complaint intake (pc_010), escalate to Product Safety

### TrailCore Grip 5 GTX Waterproofing Batch Defect (KB4: vs_028)
- **Classification:** Product defect, NOT safety hazard
- Wet feet are uncomfortable but not dangerous
- Standard warranty replacement process
- No CPSC report required

## Agent Responsibilities
1. **Never minimize a safety concern.** If customer reports overheating, smoke, fire, or injury: take it seriously regardless of product source.
2. **Follow safety complaint intake templates** (pc_010) exactly. Legal has approved specific language.
3. **Do not speculate on cause.** Say: "I'm documenting this for our safety team to investigate."
4. **Request the product** for inspection when possible (prepaid shipping label + account credit).
5. **Distinguish genuine vs. counterfeit:** A safety incident on a genuine SwiftPace is handled differently than on a PeakForm counterfeit. Verify purchase source first.
