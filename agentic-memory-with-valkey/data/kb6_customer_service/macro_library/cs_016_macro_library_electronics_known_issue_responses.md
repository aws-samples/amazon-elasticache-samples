### MACRO: AeroTrack Left Earbud Battery Drain (ts_019)
**ID:** ELEC-KI-002
**Channel:** Chat / Email
**Severity:** High

> Hi [Customer Name],
>
> Thanks for reaching out. We're aware of a battery drain issue affecting the left earbud on some AeroTrack Pro units running firmware v2.4.0. Our engineering team has identified the cause and a fix is coming in the next firmware update.
>
> In the meantime, here are two options:
> 1. **Quick fix:** In the app, go to Sound Modes → select "Off" (this stops the ANC mic from staying active)
> 2. **Full fix:** Downgrade to firmware v2.3.2 — in the app, go to Devices → AeroTrack Pro → Firmware → Advanced → Install Previous Version
>
> Either option should restore normal battery life. The permanent fix will arrive automatically with the next update.
>
> [Agent Name]
