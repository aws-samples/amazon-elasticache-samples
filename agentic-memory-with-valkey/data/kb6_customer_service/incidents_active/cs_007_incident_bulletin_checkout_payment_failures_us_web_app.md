# Incident Bulletin: Intermittent Checkout Payment Failures (US Web/App)
**Incident ID:** INC-PAY-2026-02-23-A  
**Status:** Active  
**Severity:** High  
**Effective Window:** 2026-02-23 09:10 PT to 2026-02-24 02:00 PT (subject to update)

## Summary
Some customers may experience intermittent payment failures during checkout on US web and app channels.

## Customer Symptoms
- Payment declined message despite valid card
- Checkout error after submitting payment
- Duplicate authorization concern (pending charges)
- Checkout retry eventually succeeds

## Scope
- Region: US
- Channels: Web, App
- Not confirmed for in-store POS
- Not confirmed for seller-fulfilled marketplace off-platform payment flows

## Agent Guidance
- Check incident status before troubleshooting payment method details
- Acknowledge known intermittent issue
- Avoid asking customer to repeatedly retry many times in a short period
- If duplicate pending charge is reported, follow payment concern SOP and explain pending vs posted charges carefully
- Escalate urgent billing harm cases per billing escalation workflow

## Approved Customer Messaging (Short Form)
"We are currently seeing intermittent checkout payment issues affecting some customers. I can help review your order attempt status and next-step options."

## What Not to Say
- Do not state a root cause unless listed in the bulletin
- Do not guarantee exact resolution time
- Do not advise customer to submit repeated retries without guidance

## Override Effect
This bulletin temporarily overrides standard payment troubleshooting assumptions for in-scope channels during the active window.
