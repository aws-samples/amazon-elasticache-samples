# Incident Bulletin: PeakForm Counterfeit Seller Suspension — Customer Outreach

**Incident ID:** INC-MKT-PEAKFORM-SUSPEND-2026-02
**Status:** Active (refunds in progress)
**Severity:** High
**Opened:** 2026-02-15
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-24

## Summary
PeakForm Athletics (PFA-US-720) suspended for selling counterfeit Accent Athletics products. 47 authenticity complaints, 1 safety incident (counterfeit SwiftPace overheating). All PeakForm listings removed. Refund program active.

## Customer Impact
- Estimated 1,200 customers purchased from PeakForm in the last 6 months
- Proactive email sent to all PeakForm customers on Feb 18 offering refunds
- ~40% of customers have responded and requested refunds so far
- Remaining 60% may contact support as they become aware

## Agent Actions
1. **Identify:** Check if order seller is PeakForm / PFA-US-720
2. **Communicate:** Use approved language from KB5: pc_024 and macros MKT-002/MKT-003 (cs_017)
3. **Refund:** Process per KB5: pc_021 exception (full refund regardless of purchase date for last 90 days, manual process for 90–180 days)
4. **Safety:** If electronics product, provide safety advisory. If customer reports overheating/damage, escalate per KB5: pc_017
5. **Redirect:** Offer to help find genuine product from authorized sellers (RunReady for footwear, FitTech for electronics, TrailBound for trail gear)
6. **Do NOT troubleshoot** PeakForm products — they are not genuine

## Proactive Email (Already Sent Feb 18)
Subject: "Important information about your recent Accent Athletics marketplace purchase"
- Informed customers that seller was removed
- Offered one-click refund link
- Included safety advisory for electronics purchases
- Did NOT use the word "counterfeit" in the email
