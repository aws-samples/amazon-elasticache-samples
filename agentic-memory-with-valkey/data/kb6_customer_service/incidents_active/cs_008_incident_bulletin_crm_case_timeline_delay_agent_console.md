# Incident Bulletin: CRM Case Timeline Delay (Agent Console)
**Incident ID:** INC-CRM-2026-02-22-B  
**Status:** Active  
**Severity:** Medium  
**Effective Window:** 2026-02-22 13:20 PT to 2026-02-23 18:00 PT (subject to update)

## Summary
Agent console case timelines may show delayed updates. New notes and status changes can take several minutes to appear.

## Impact to Agents
- Case notes may not appear immediately
- Recent macro usage may not be visible right away
- Duplicate note entry risk increases

## Agent Workarounds
- Wait and refresh before re-entering the same case note
- Confirm save/submit acknowledgment where available
- Avoid duplicate escalations if status appears stale for a short interval

## Customer Communication Guidance
Do not reference internal CRM issue details unless needed to explain a callback delay. Use simple language:
- "I am documenting this for follow-up and there may be a short delay before the update appears in our system."

## Override Effect
This bulletin modifies standard case documentation timing expectations while active.
