# Announcement: Goodwill Credit Limits Updated — Effective Feb 1, 2026

**Status:** Active
**Published:** 2026-02-01
**Audience:** All US Support Agents

## What Changed
The goodwill credit policy has been updated. The old $10 maximum for Tier 1 agents (pc_029 — now deprecated) has been replaced:

| Agent Level | Old Limit | New Limit |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | $10 | **$15** |
| Tier 2 | $25 | **$50** |
| CX Manager | $100 | **$200** |

## Why
Customer issues are more complex now (firmware bricking, false health alerts, counterfeit products). The old $10 limit forced unnecessary escalations and made customers feel undervalued.

## Active Exceptions (Override Standard Limits)
Some current issues have temporary exception policies that allow higher credits:
- **SleepSync apnea alerts (pc_023):** Tier 1 up to $50, Tier 2 up to $200
- **SwiftPace bricking (ts_022):** $25 credit + free replacement
- **AeroTrack battery drain (ts_019):** $15 at agent discretion

## How to Apply
- Use credit code in support portal as usual
- For exception credits, use the specific exception code (e.g., SLEEPSYNC-GW-2026, BRICK-2026)
- Credits appear on customer's account within 24 hours

## Common Mistake
If you were trained before Feb 2026, you may have the $10 limit memorized. **Update your mental model.** The new limit is $15 standard, higher with exceptions. Offering only $10 when you could offer $15 leaves value on the table and may result in unnecessary escalations.
