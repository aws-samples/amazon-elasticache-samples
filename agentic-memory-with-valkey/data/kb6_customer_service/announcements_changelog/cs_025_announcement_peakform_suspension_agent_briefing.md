# Announcement: PeakForm Seller Suspension — Agent Briefing

**Status:** Active
**Published:** 2026-02-16
**Audience:** All US Support Agents

## What Happened
PeakForm Athletics (seller ID PFA-US-720) has been suspended from the Accent Athletics marketplace effective Feb 15, 2026 for selling products that did not meet our authenticity standards.

## What You Need to Know
1. **PeakForm listings are removed.** No new purchases possible.
2. **~1,200 customers may contact us.** Proactive email sent Feb 18 but not all customers will read it.
3. **Full refunds authorized** for all PeakForm purchases in the last 90 days. No questions asked. See KB5: pc_021.
4. **Do NOT troubleshoot PeakForm products.** They are not genuine Accent Athletics products. KB3 troubleshooting guides do not apply.
5. **Safety concern for electronics.** One overheating incident reported. If customer has PeakForm electronics, advise to stop using. See KB5: pc_017.
6. **Use approved language only.** See KB5: pc_024 and macros in cs_017. Do NOT say "counterfeit" or "fake" unless customer says it first.

## Quick Reference Card
- Customer has PeakForm order → Refund (pc_021) + Safety advisory if electronics (pc_017)
- Customer asks "is it fake?" → Use pc_024 Template 2
- Customer reports safety issue → Use pc_024 Template 3 + escalate to Product Safety
- Customer wants genuine product → Direct to accentathletics.com or authorized sellers (RunReady, FitTech, TrailBound)

## Training
A 15-minute training module on counterfeit handling has been added to the learning portal. All agents should complete by Feb 28.
