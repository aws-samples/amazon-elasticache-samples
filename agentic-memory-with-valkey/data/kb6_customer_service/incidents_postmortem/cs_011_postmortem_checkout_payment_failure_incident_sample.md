# Postmortem Summary: Checkout Payment Failure Incident (Sample)
**Incident ID:** INC-PAY-2026-02-23-A  
**Status:** Closed  
**Postmortem Date:** 2026-02-24

## Summary
Intermittent checkout payment failures affected a subset of US web/app transactions during the incident window.

## Customer Impact (Sample)
- Increased checkout failures
- Retry behavior increased contact volume
- Some customers reported duplicate pending authorizations

## Agent Learnings
- Early incident acknowledgment reduced unnecessary troubleshooting
- Macro consistency improved customer messaging quality
- Duplicate pending authorization questions should be addressed with standard billing explanation language
