# SOP: Contact Handling and Triage Workflow (US)

## Purpose
Provide a consistent first-response and triage workflow for customer support contacts across chat, email, and phone channels.

## Scope
Applies to:
- Order, shipping, returns, and refund questions
- Product support and troubleshooting intake
- Marketplace seller disputes
- Safety complaints
- Policy clarification requests

## Triage Workflow (Baseline)
1. **Identify issue type**
   - Order/shipping
   - Return/refund/exchange
   - Product troubleshooting
   - Marketplace seller order
   - Safety complaint
   - Privacy/data request
   - Billing/payment issue

2. **Confirm key identifiers**
   - Order ID (if applicable)
   - Product / SKU
   - Customer contact channel
   - Seller/fulfillment ownership (for marketplace)
   - Region

3. **Check active incidents/outages first**
   Before giving troubleshooting or workaround steps, review active incident bulletins for:
   - checkout/payment issues
   - CRM/agent console delays
   - order status sync delays
   - regional service disruptions

4. **Use the right knowledge source**
   - Policies & Compliance KB for rules
   - Product Knowledge KB for product details/fit/recommendations
   - Troubleshooting & Support History KB for technical issues
   - Vendor & Seller KB for seller ownership/policy
   - Store Operations KB for local store constraints

5. **Respond using approved macro + brand voice**
   - Personalize facts
   - Do not over-promise timelines or outcomes
   - Use legal-approved wording when required

6. **Escalate when trigger conditions are met**
   - safety issue
   - policy/legal dispute
   - missed seller SLA
   - repeated unresolved failure
   - system outage impacts resolution

## Special Handling Paths

### Safety complaints
Use urgent safety intake flow and approved safety wording. Do not ask customer to continue using the product.

### Privacy requests
Use privacy intake templates and route through privacy workflow. Do not confirm completion during first contact.

### Marketplace orders
Confirm fulfillment ownership before quoting return or shipping policy.

## Override Precedence
When guidance conflicts, use:
1. Active incident bulletin
2. Temporary policy exception
3. Official policy / seller-specific policy
4. SOP workflow
5. Macro template
