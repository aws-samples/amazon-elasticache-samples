# Accent Pro

## Overview
The Accent Pro is a versatile lifestyle sneaker built for all-day wear in unpredictable weather. It features a DWR (Durable Water Repellent) treated upper that sheds light rain and splashes, keeping feet dry during commutes and casual outings. Note: the Accent Pro is water resistant, not waterproof — it will not protect against prolonged submersion or heavy downpours.

## Best For
- Daily commuting in light rain or damp conditions
- Casual all-day wear with weather protection
- Customers who want a sneaker that transitions from indoors to outdoors
- Light trail walks on dewy mornings

## Key Features
- DWR-treated mesh and synthetic upper repels light moisture
- Sealed seams at toe cap and heel for splash protection
- Cushioned EVA midsole for all-day comfort
- Rubber outsole with wet-grip tread pattern
- Reflective heel tab for low-light visibility
- Removable OrthoLite insole

## Water Resistance Details
- Rated: Water Resistant (IPX-4 equivalent splash protection)
- NOT rated: Waterproof — will not withstand standing water, heavy rain, or submersion
- DWR coating effectiveness diminishes over time; re-treat with spray-on DWR every 3-6 months
- For fully waterproof trail use, consider the TrailCore Grip 5 GTX (SKU: FW-028)

## Fit Notes
- Standard fit through heel and midfoot
- Slightly roomier toe box than AeroFlex Runner 2
- Runs true to size for most customers
- Available in men's and women's sizing

## Materials
- Upper: DWR-treated engineered mesh / synthetic overlays
- Midsole: Cushioned EVA foam
- Outsole: Wet-grip rubber compound
- Insole: Removable OrthoLite
- Lining: Moisture-wicking textile

## Care
- Wipe clean with damp cloth
- Re-apply DWR spray every 3-6 months for best water resistance
- Air dry only — do not machine wash or tumble dry
- Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight when drying
