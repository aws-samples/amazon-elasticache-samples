# CloudWalk Recovery Slide

## Overview
CloudWalk Recovery Slide is a post-workout recovery sandal with a thick foam footbed designed to give tired feet a break after runs and training sessions.

## Best For
- Post-run recovery
- Casual wear around the house or gym
- Customers with plantar fasciitis seeking soft support

## Key Features
- 30mm thick molded foam footbed
- Contoured arch support
- Textured footbed surface for gentle massage effect
- Lightweight single-piece construction
- Slip-on design

## Fit Notes
- Runs true to size; unisex sizing available
- Relaxed open-toe fit
- Not intended for running or high-impact activity

## Materials
- Upper: Synthetic strap
- Footbed: Injection-molded EVA foam
- Outsole: Textured EVA

## Care
- Rinse with water and mild soap
- Air dry
