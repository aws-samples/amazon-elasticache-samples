# TrailCore Grip 5 GTX

## Overview
Waterproof version of the TrailCore Grip 5 with a GORE-TEX membrane for wet-weather trail running. Same outsole and protection features with added waterproof breathability.

## Best For
- Trail running in rain, snow, and creek crossings
- Wet-weather hiking
- Customers who need waterproof trail footwear

## Key Features
- All features of TrailCore Grip 5
- GORE-TEX Invisible Fit membrane for waterproof breathability
- Sealed seams
- Slightly heavier than non-GTX version (+1.2 oz per shoe)

## Fit Notes
- Same fit as TrailCore Grip 5
- GORE-TEX liner may feel slightly warmer in hot conditions
- Not recommended for summer trail running in warm climates

## Materials
- Upper: Ripstop mesh with GORE-TEX membrane
- Midsole: Dual-density EVA foam
- Outsole: Vibram rubber compound

## Care
- Rinse mud off with cold water
- Air dry; do not use direct heat
- Reapply DWR spray periodically for best performance
