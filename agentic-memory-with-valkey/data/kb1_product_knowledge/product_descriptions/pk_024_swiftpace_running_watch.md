# SwiftPace Running Watch

## Overview
GPS running watch with heart rate monitor, pace alerts, and 7-day battery life. Simple interface focused on run tracking without smartwatch distractions.

## Best For
- Runners who want GPS pace and distance tracking
- Training plan followers who need interval and lap timers
- Customers who prefer a dedicated running device over a smartwatch

## Key Features
- Built-in GPS with GLONASS
- Optical wrist heart rate monitor
- Customizable pace, distance, and heart rate alerts
- Interval and lap timer
- 7-day battery (smartwatch mode) / 14 hours (GPS mode)
- 5 ATM water resistance
- Syncs with major running apps via Bluetooth

## Sizing
- One size; adjustable silicone band (fits 5.1"–8.3" wrist)

## Materials
- Case: Fiber-reinforced polymer
- Band: Silicone
- Display: Transflective MIP LCD
