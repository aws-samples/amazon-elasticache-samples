# GripLite Running Gloves

## Overview
Lightweight touchscreen-compatible running gloves for cool-weather training. Silicone grip pads on palm and fingers for secure hold on phone or water bottle.

## Best For
- Running in 35–50°F conditions
- Cycling and outdoor cardio
- Customers who need phone access while wearing gloves

## Key Features
- Touchscreen-compatible thumb and index finger
- Silicone grip pads on palm
- Moisture-wicking brushed interior
- Reflective logo
- Lightweight and packable

## Sizing
- Available: S/M, L/XL
- Snug fit recommended for best touchscreen response

## Materials
- 85% polyester / 15% elastane with silicone grip
- Machine wash cold, air dry
