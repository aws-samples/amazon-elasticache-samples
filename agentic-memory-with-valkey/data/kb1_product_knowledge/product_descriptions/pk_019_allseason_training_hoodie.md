# AllSeason Training Hoodie

## Overview
Midweight pullover hoodie for warm-ups, cool-downs, and casual wear. Brushed fleece interior with athletic cut that works for gym and street.

## Best For
- Pre/post workout layering
- Cool-weather outdoor walks
- Casual everyday wear

## Key Features
- Brushed fleece interior for warmth
- Kangaroo pocket with internal media port
- Three-panel hood with drawcord
- Ribbed cuffs and hem
- Athletic fit through torso

## Sizing
- Available: XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL
- Fit: Athletic; not oversized

## Materials
- 60% cotton / 40% recycled polyester
- Machine wash cold, tumble dry low
