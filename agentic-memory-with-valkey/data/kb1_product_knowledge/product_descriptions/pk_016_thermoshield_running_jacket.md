# ThermoShield Running Jacket

## Overview
Lightweight wind- and water-resistant running jacket for cool and wet conditions. Packable design stows into its own pocket for easy carry.

## Best For
- Running in rain and wind
- Cool-weather outdoor training
- Layering for variable conditions

## Key Features
- DWR (durable water repellent) finish
- Windproof front panel
- Mesh-lined back vent for breathability
- Packable into chest pocket
- Reflective trim on front, back, and sleeves
- Elastic cuffs with thumb loops

## Sizing
- Available: XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL
- Fit: Slim athletic; designed to layer over a tee
- Weight: 5.8 oz (men's M)

## Materials
- Shell: 100% recycled nylon with DWR
- Lining: Mesh panels
- Machine wash cold, hang dry; reapply DWR after 20 washes
