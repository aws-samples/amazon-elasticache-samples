# TempoRun Interval Trainer

## Overview
Lightweight training shoe designed for speed workouts, tempo runs, and track sessions. Sits between the daily trainer (AeroFlex Runner 2) and the race flat (SprintEdge Racer) in cushioning and weight.

## Best For
- Tempo runs and interval workouts
- Track sessions
- Runners who want a faster-feeling trainer without going full race flat

## Key Features
- Responsive Pebax foam midsole
- Lightweight mesh upper (8.1 oz men's size 10)
- Moderate rocker geometry for quick transitions
- Thin rubber outsole for ground feel
- No carbon plate (unlike SprintEdge Racer)

## Fit Notes
- True to size; snugger than AeroFlex Runner 2
- Narrower heel cup for lockdown at speed
- Not ideal for wide feet

## Materials
- Upper: Engineered mesh
- Midsole: Pebax foam
- Outsole: Rubber

## Care
- Spot clean; air dry
