# FlexForm Training Shoe

## Overview
FlexForm Training Shoe is a versatile cross-trainer for gym workouts, HIIT classes, and light running. Flat stable base for lifting with enough cushion for cardio circuits.

## Best For
- Gym and weight training
- HIIT and circuit classes
- Customers who want one shoe for mixed gym use

## Key Features
- Wide flat outsole for stable lifting platform
- Flexible forefoot for agility drills
- Breathable mesh upper with midfoot strap for lockdown
- Low-profile cushioning (not a running shoe)
- Reinforced sidewalls for lateral movement

## Fit Notes
- True to size
- Standard width; midfoot strap adjusts snugness
- Firmer feel than running shoes by design

## Materials
- Upper: Mesh / synthetic with TPU midfoot strap
- Midsole: Firm EVA foam
- Outsole: Flat rubber with hex-grip pattern

## Care
- Spot clean with damp cloth
- Air dry; remove insoles after sweaty sessions
