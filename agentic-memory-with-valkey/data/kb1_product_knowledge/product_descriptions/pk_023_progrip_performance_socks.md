# ProGrip Performance Socks (3-Pack)

## Overview
Cushioned crew-length running socks with arch compression and blister-resistant construction. Sold in 3-pack.

## Best For
- Road and trail running
- Gym and cross-training
- Everyday athletic wear

## Key Features
- Targeted cushioning in heel and forefoot
- Arch compression band for support
- Mesh ventilation zones on top of foot
- Seamless toe closure to reduce blister risk
- Left/right anatomical fit

## Sizing
- Available: S (W 5–7 / M 3.5–5.5), M (W 7.5–10 / M 6–8.5), L (W 10.5–12 / M 9–12), XL (M 12.5–15)

## Materials
- 65% merino wool / 25% nylon / 7% polyester / 3% elastane
- Machine wash warm, tumble dry low
