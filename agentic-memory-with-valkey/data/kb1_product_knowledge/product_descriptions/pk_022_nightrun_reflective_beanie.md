# NightRun Reflective Beanie

## Overview
Lightweight running beanie with 360-degree reflective yarn for visibility during early morning and evening runs. Moisture-wicking and wind-resistant.

## Best For
- Cold-weather running (25–45°F)
- Low-light and nighttime visibility
- Cycling and outdoor commuting

## Key Features
- 360-degree reflective yarn woven throughout
- Wind-resistant front panel
- Moisture-wicking interior
- Ponytail-compatible slot in back
- One size fits most

## Materials
- 70% acrylic / 20% reflective yarn / 10% elastane
- Hand wash cold, lay flat to dry
