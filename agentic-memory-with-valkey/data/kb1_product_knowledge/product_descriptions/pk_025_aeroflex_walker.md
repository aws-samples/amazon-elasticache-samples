# AeroFlex Walker

## Overview
Walking-specific shoe based on the AeroFlex platform with enhanced arch support and a slightly wider fit for all-day comfort. Designed for customers who walk for fitness or spend long hours on their feet.

## Best For
- Fitness walking
- All-day standing and walking
- Customers who want AeroFlex comfort without a running-specific design

## Key Features
- Enhanced arch support insert
- Slightly wider last than AeroFlex Runner 2
- Durable rubber outsole for pavement and indoor surfaces
- Padded collar and tongue
- Clean casual styling

## Fit Notes
- True to size; slightly roomier than AeroFlex Runner 2
- Suitable for most standard and slightly wide feet
- Customers with very wide feet should consider AeroFlex Runner 2 Wide

## Materials
- Upper: Engineered mesh / leather-look synthetic overlays
- Midsole: EVA foam with arch support insert
- Outsole: Rubber

## Care
- Spot clean with mild soap
- Air dry
