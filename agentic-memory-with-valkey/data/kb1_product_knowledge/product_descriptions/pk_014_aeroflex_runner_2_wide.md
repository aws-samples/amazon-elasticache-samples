# AeroFlex Runner 2 Wide

## Overview
Same platform and cushioning as the AeroFlex Runner 2 with a roomier forefoot and toe box to accommodate wider feet. Identical midsole, outsole, and upper materials.

## Best For
- Customers with wide feet or bunions
- Customers who found standard AeroFlex Runner 2 too snug in the forefoot
- Daily road running and walking

## Key Features
- All features of AeroFlex Runner 2
- Wider last in forefoot and toe box area
- Same weight and stack height as standard version

## Fit Notes
- Wide fit in forefoot; standard in heel
- Recommended for customers who typically buy wide or EE shoes
- If standard AeroFlex Runner 2 fits well, this version may feel too loose

## Materials
- Identical to AeroFlex Runner 2

## Care
- Spot clean with mild soap and cold water
- Air dry only
