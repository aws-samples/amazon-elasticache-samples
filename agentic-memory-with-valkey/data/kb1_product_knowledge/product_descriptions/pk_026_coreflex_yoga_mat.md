# CoreFlex Yoga Mat

## Overview
Non-slip 6mm yoga and stretching mat with alignment lines and carrying strap. Suitable for yoga, Pilates, and floor exercises.

## Best For
- Yoga and Pilates
- Post-run stretching
- Home gym floor work

## Key Features
- 6mm thickness for joint cushioning
- Laser-etched alignment lines
- Non-slip textured surface on both sides
- Carrying strap included
- Dimensions: 72" x 26"

## Materials
- TPE (thermoplastic elastomer); PVC-free, latex-free
- Wipe clean with damp cloth; air dry flat
