# SprintEdge Racer

## Overview
SprintEdge Racer is a lightweight race-day flat designed for 5K to half-marathon distances. Minimal upper, carbon-fiber plate in the midsole, and aggressive rocker geometry for fast turnover.

## Best For
- Race day (5K through half marathon)
- Speed workouts and tempo runs
- Experienced runners seeking a performance edge

## Key Features
- Carbon-fiber plate for propulsive toe-off
- Ultra-light engineered mesh upper (under 7 oz men's size 10)
- High-rebound nitrogen-infused foam midsole
- Thin rubber outsole pods in high-wear zones
- Internal heel counter for lockdown at speed

## Fit Notes
- Runs true to size in length
- Snug, race-fit feel through midfoot
- Not recommended for wide feet; no wide option available

## Materials
- Upper: Ultra-light engineered mesh
- Midsole: Nitrogen-infused foam with carbon-fiber plate
- Outsole: Lightweight rubber pods

## Care
- Spot clean only
- Reserve for race and speed sessions to maximize lifespan
