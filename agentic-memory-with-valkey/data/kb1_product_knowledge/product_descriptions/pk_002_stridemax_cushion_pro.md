# StrideMax Cushion Pro

## Overview
StrideMax Cushion Pro is built for runners who prioritize comfort on long runs and recovery days. It features a high-stack cushioned platform and a smooth rocker shape to help reduce underfoot fatigue during extended wear.

## Best For
- Long-distance road running
- Recovery runs
- Customers who prefer soft cushioning

## Key Features
- Max-cushion midsole for soft landings
- Rocker geometry for smoother heel-to-toe transitions
- Knit upper with padded tongue
- Wider base for added stability
- Durable outsole with full-length traction coverage

## Fit Notes
- True to size in length for most customers
- Slightly roomier than AeroFlex Runner 2 in the forefoot
- Customers who like a snug race fit may prefer AeroFlex Runner 2

## Materials
- Upper: Knit textile / synthetic reinforcement
- Midsole: High-rebound foam blend
- Outsole: Rubber

## Care
- Spot clean only
- Remove insoles to air out after use
