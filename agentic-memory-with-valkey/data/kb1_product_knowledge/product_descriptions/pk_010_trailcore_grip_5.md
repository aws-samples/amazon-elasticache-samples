# TrailCore Grip 5

## Overview
TrailCore Grip 5 is a rugged trail running shoe built for mixed terrain, gravel paths, and light technical trails. It features aggressive outsole lugs, a reinforced toe bumper, and a protective rock plate for confident footing on uneven ground.

## Best For
- Trail running on dirt, gravel, and rocky paths
- Hiking and mixed-terrain outings
- Runners transitioning from road to trail

## Key Features
- Multi-directional lug outsole for grip on loose and wet surfaces
- TPU toe bumper for rock and root protection
- Internal rock plate to shield against sharp underfoot objects
- Gusseted tongue to keep debris out
- Quick-pull lace system for secure lockdown

## Fit Notes
- True to size for most customers
- Standard width; structured upper feels more secure than road models
- Slightly heavier than AeroFlex Runner 2 due to protective features

## Materials
- Upper: Ripstop mesh / synthetic overlays
- Midsole: Dual-density EVA foam
- Outsole: Vibram rubber compound
- Lining: Moisture-wicking textile

## Care
- Rinse mud off with cold water after trail use
- Air dry away from direct heat
- Do not machine wash
