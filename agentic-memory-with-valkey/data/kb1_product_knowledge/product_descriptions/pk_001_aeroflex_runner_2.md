# AeroFlex Runner 2

## Overview
The AeroFlex Runner 2 is a lightweight daily running shoe designed for road miles, walking, and everyday wear. It combines a breathable engineered mesh upper with medium cushioning for a balanced ride that feels responsive without being overly firm.

## Best For
- Daily road running (short to medium distances)
- Walking and all-day casual wear
- Runners who prefer a lighter shoe with moderate cushioning

## Key Features
- Engineered mesh upper for airflow
- Mid-cushion foam midsole for balanced comfort
- Durable rubber outsole in high-wear zones
- Padded heel collar for step-in comfort
- Gusseted tongue to reduce tongue shift

## Fit Notes
- Standard fit through heel and midfoot
- Forefoot fits true to size for most customers
- Customers with wider forefoot or bunions may prefer AeroFlex Runner 2 Wide

## Materials
- Upper: Engineered mesh / synthetic overlays
- Midsole: EVA blend foam
- Outsole: Rubber
- Lining: Textile

## Care
- Spot clean with mild soap and cold water
- Air dry only
- Do not machine wash or machine dry
