# FlexStride Running Short 7"

## Overview
Versatile 7-inch inseam running short with built-in brief liner, zippered back pocket, and lightweight woven fabric.

## Best For
- Road and trail running
- Gym cardio
- Warm-weather training

## Key Features
- Built-in brief liner for support
- Zippered back pocket fits most phones
- Side split hem for range of motion
- Elastic waistband with internal drawcord
- Reflective logo

## Sizing
- Available: XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL
- Inseam: 7 inches
- Fit: Standard athletic

## Materials
- Shell: 100% recycled polyester
- Liner: Polyester/elastane blend
- Machine wash cold, tumble dry low
