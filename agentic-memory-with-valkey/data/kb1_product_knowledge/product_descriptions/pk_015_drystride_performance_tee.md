# DryStride Performance Tee

## Overview
Lightweight moisture-wicking crew-neck tee for running, gym, and active use. Quick-dry fabric with flatlock seams to reduce chafing on long runs.

## Best For
- Running and cardio
- Gym training
- Warm-weather outdoor activity

## Key Features
- Moisture-wicking polyester blend fabric
- Flatlock seams throughout
- Reflective logo detail for low-light visibility
- Relaxed athletic fit
- UPF 30 sun protection

## Sizing
- Available: XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL
- Fit: Relaxed athletic; not compression
- Length: Standard; covers waistband

## Materials
- 92% recycled polyester / 8% elastane
- Machine wash cold, tumble dry low

## Colors
- Black, Navy, Heather Grey, Signal Green, White
