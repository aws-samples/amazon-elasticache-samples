# SprintEdge Racer Review Summary (US)

## Aggregate Snapshot
- Average rating: 4.6 / 5
- Total reviews analyzed: 87
- Summary window: Last 12 months

## What Customers Like Most
1. Noticeably faster feel; multiple customers reported PRs
2. Carbon plate gives strong toe-off pop
3. Incredibly light on foot
4. Looks fast

## Common Complaints
1. Durability is limited; outsole wears after 150–200 miles
2. Not comfortable for easy runs or daily training
3. No wide option; narrow fit excludes some customers
4. Price point is high for a limited-use shoe

## Fit Theme Summary
- True to size in length
- Snug race fit; not for wide feet
- Heel lockdown is excellent at speed
