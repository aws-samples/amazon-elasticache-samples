# StrideMax Cushion Pro Review Summary (US)

## Aggregate Snapshot
- Average rating: 4.4 / 5
- Total reviews analyzed: 221
- Summary window: Last 12 months

## What Customers Like Most
1. Cloud-like cushioning; feels like walking on pillows
2. Rocker geometry makes long runs feel effortless
3. Roomier forefoot than AeroFlex Runner 2
4. Great recovery day shoe

## Common Complaints
1. Heavier than expected; not ideal for speed work
2. Rocker shape feels unstable to some first-time users
3. Midsole compresses after 350–400 miles
4. Limited color options

## Fit Theme Summary
- True to size in length
- Slightly roomier than AeroFlex Runner 2 in forefoot
- Customers who like a snug race fit find it too loose

## Durability Theme Summary
- Outsole holds up well on road surfaces
- Midsole cushioning degrades noticeably after 400 miles

## Representative Review Snippets
- "My knees feel so much better after switching to these for long runs."
- "Takes a run or two to get used to the rocker but then it clicks."
- "I use AeroFlex for short runs and StrideMax for anything over 8 miles."
