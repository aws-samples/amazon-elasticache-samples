# TrailCore Grip 5 Review Summary (US)

## Aggregate Snapshot
- Average rating: 4.5 / 5
- Total reviews analyzed: 194
- Summary window: Last 12 months

## What Customers Like Most
1. Excellent grip on wet rocks and loose gravel
2. Toe bumper saved toes on rocky trails multiple times
3. Comfortable for long trail days (15+ miles)
4. Gusseted tongue keeps debris out effectively

## Common Complaints
1. Heavier than expected compared to road shoes
2. Lug pattern picks up mud in very soft terrain
3. Break-in period of 2–3 runs for some customers

## Fit Theme Summary
- Most customers report true to size
- Structured upper feels more secure than road shoes
- A few customers with very wide feet found it snug

## Durability Theme Summary
- Vibram outsole holds up well over 300+ miles
- Upper mesh shows wear at toe flex point for heavy-mileage users

## Representative Review Snippets
- "Best trail shoe I've owned. Grip is incredible on wet granite."
- "A bit heavy for fast trail runs but perfect for technical terrain."
- "Toe bumper is a lifesaver on rocky New England trails."
