# CoreFlex Yoga Mat Review Summary (US)

## Aggregate Snapshot
- Average rating: 4.3 / 5
- Total reviews analyzed: 156
- Summary window: Last 12 months

## What Customers Like Most
1. Non-slip surface works well even with sweaty hands
2. Alignment lines help with pose positioning
3. 6mm thickness is comfortable on hard floors
4. PVC-free and no chemical smell out of the box

## Common Complaints
1. Heavier than travel mats; not ideal for carrying long distances
2. Carrying strap is basic; a bag would be better
3. Surface shows footprints and marks easily
4. Edges curl slightly when new; flattens after a week

- "Great grip even in hot yoga. No slipping."
