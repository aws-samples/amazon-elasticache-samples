# SwiftPace Running Watch Review Summary (US)

## Aggregate Snapshot
- Average rating: 4.2 / 5
- Total reviews analyzed: 156
- Summary window: Last 12 months

## What Customers Like Most
1. Battery life is outstanding; GPS lasts full marathon with room to spare
2. Simple interface; no smartwatch bloat
3. GPS accuracy is solid after firmware update
4. Syncs easily with Strava and Garmin Connect

## Common Complaints
1. Screen is hard to read in direct sunlight at certain angles
2. Heart rate monitor less accurate during intervals vs steady state
3. No music storage or phone notifications
4. Charging cable is proprietary

## Fit Theme Summary
- Band fits most wrists comfortably
- Some customers with very small wrists find it slightly large

- "Finally a running watch that just does running. No distractions."
- "Battery lasted my entire 50K ultra with 40% left."
- "HR readings jump around during speed work. Fine for easy runs."
