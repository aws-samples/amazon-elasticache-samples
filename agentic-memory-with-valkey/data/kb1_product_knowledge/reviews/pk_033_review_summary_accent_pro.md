# Accent Pro Review Summary (US)

## Aggregate Snapshot
- Average rating: 4.4 / 5
- Total reviews analyzed: 142
- Summary window: Last 12 months

## What Customers Like Most
1. Handles light rain and puddle splashes well — feet stay dry during commutes
2. Comfortable enough for all-day wear at work or on weekends
3. Clean, versatile look that pairs with jeans or chinos
4. Wet-grip outsole feels secure on slick sidewalks and tile floors

## Common Complaints
1. Not waterproof — several customers expected full waterproofing and were disappointed in heavy rain
2. DWR coating wears off after a few months without re-treatment
3. A few customers noted the shoe runs slightly warm in summer due to the DWR-treated upper
4. Limited color options compared to other lifestyle sneakers

## Water Resistance Theme Summary
- Majority of positive reviews specifically praise performance in light rain and morning dew
- Negative reviews almost always stem from misunderstanding "water resistant" vs "waterproof"
- Customers who re-apply DWR spray report sustained performance over 6+ months
- Support agents should clarify the distinction proactively when customers ask about wet-weather use

## Fit Theme Summary
- Most customers report "true to size"
- Toe box is slightly roomier than AeroFlex Runner 2, which wide-foot customers appreciate
- No significant complaints about heel slip or tongue shift

## Durability Theme Summary
- Outsole holds up well on pavement and light trails
- Upper shows minimal wear after 3-4 months of daily use
- DWR coating is the main maintenance item — not a durability defect
