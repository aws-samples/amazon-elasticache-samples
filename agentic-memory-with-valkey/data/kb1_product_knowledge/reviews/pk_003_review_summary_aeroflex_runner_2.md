# AeroFlex Runner 2 Review Summary (US)

## Aggregate Snapshot
- Average rating: 4.3 / 5
- Total reviews analyzed: 286
- Summary window: Last 12 months

## What Customers Like Most
1. Lightweight feel for daily runs and walking
2. Breathable upper (especially in warm weather)
3. Comfortable out of the box with minimal break-in
4. Good value for the price

## Common Complaints
1. Toe box feels narrow for some customers
2. Cushioning may feel too firm for long runs (10+ miles)
3. Heel collar rub reported by a small subset of users during early wear

## Fit Theme Summary
- Most customers report "true to size"
- Repeated fit issue: forefoot feels snug for wide feet
- Customers with wide feet often report better experience with AeroFlex Runner 2 Wide

## Durability Theme Summary
- Positive feedback on outsole wear for road use
- A minority of heavy-mileage runners reported upper wear near toe flex point after frequent use

## Representative Review Snippets (Redacted/Synthetic)
- "Very light and easy for daily walks. I bought a second pair for travel."
- "Great shoe, but I wish the toe box had a little more room."
- "Good for 5K and gym cardio. I use another shoe for longer runs."
