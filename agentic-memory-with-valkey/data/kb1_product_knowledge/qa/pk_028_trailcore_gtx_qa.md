# TrailCore Grip 5 GTX Community Q&A (Sample)

## Q1: Is this fully waterproof or just water-resistant?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** TrailCore Grip 5 GTX uses a GORE-TEX Invisible Fit membrane with sealed seams, making it fully waterproof. Water will not penetrate through the upper. However, water can enter over the ankle collar in deep puddles or stream crossings.

## Q2: Can I use these in summer?
**Answer (Community):** They run warm. I only use the GTX version from October through April. In summer I switch to the regular TrailCore Grip 5.

## Q3: What's the weight difference vs the non-GTX?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** TrailCore Grip 5 GTX is approximately 1.2 oz heavier per shoe than the standard version due to the GORE-TEX membrane and sealed seam construction.

## Q4: Do I need to treat them with waterproofing spray?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** The GORE-TEX membrane provides waterproofing. The outer fabric has a DWR coating that may need periodic reapplication (spray-on DWR) to maintain water beading on the surface. The membrane itself does not lose waterproofing.

## Q5: Are the soles the same as the regular version?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** Yes. Identical Vibram rubber outsole with the same lug pattern and rock plate.
