# DryStride Performance Tee Community Q&A (Sample)

## Q1: Does this shrink in the wash?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** DryStride is made from recycled polyester blend which resists shrinking. Machine wash cold and tumble dry low for best results.

## Q2: Is this see-through when wet?
**Answer (Community):** The darker colors (Black, Navy) are fine. White and Heather Grey can be slightly see-through when soaked with sweat.

## Q3: Does it have a pocket?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** No. DryStride Performance Tee does not have pockets. For secure storage while running, we recommend FlexStride Running Short which has a zippered back pocket.

## Q4: Is the UPF rating real?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** Yes. DryStride is rated UPF 30, which blocks approximately 97% of UV radiation. For maximum sun protection, we recommend also wearing SunShield Running Cap.

## Q5: Can I use this for swimming or water sports?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** While the fabric dries quickly, DryStride is designed for land-based activities. It is not a rash guard and does not provide the same level of UV protection when wet.
