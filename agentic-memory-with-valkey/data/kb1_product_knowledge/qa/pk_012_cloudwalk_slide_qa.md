# CloudWalk Recovery Slide Community Q&A (Sample)

## Q1: Can I wear these in the shower?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** The slides are water-friendly and can be worn in showers or pool areas. Note that the footbed texture may be slippery on very wet tile surfaces.

## Q2: Do these help with plantar fasciitis?
**Answer (Community):** They helped me a lot. The arch support and thick cushion take pressure off the heel. Not a medical device but definitely more supportive than flat flip-flops.

## Q3: Can I run in these?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** No. CloudWalk Recovery Slides are designed for post-activity recovery and casual wear only. They are not intended for running or high-impact activity.

## Q4: Do they come in half sizes?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** Currently available in whole sizes only. If you are between sizes, we recommend sizing down for a snugger fit.

## Q5: Do they smell after a while?
**Answer (Community):** Mine started to smell after a few months of daily use. Rinsing with soap and water and air drying fixes it.
