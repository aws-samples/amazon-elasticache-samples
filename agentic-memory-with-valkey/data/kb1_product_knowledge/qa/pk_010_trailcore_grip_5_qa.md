# TrailCore Grip 5 Community Q&A (Sample)

## Q1: Can I use these on pavement too?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** TrailCore Grip 5 is optimized for trail surfaces. The lugs will wear faster on pavement. For mixed road/trail use, consider alternating with a road shoe like AeroFlex Runner 2.

## Q2: Are these waterproof?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** The standard TrailCore Grip 5 is water-resistant but not waterproof. For full waterproof protection, see TrailCore Grip 5 GTX which includes a GORE-TEX membrane.

## Q3: How do these compare to AeroFlex Runner 2 for weight?
**Answer (Community):** They're noticeably heavier — about 2 oz more per shoe. You feel the extra protection and outsole. Worth it on trails though.

## Q4: Do the lugs work on ice?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** The Vibram outsole provides good grip on wet and loose surfaces but is not designed for ice. For icy conditions, consider traction devices that attach over the shoe.

## Q5: Is there a wide version?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** TrailCore Grip 5 is currently available in standard width only. Customers with wider feet report the structured upper accommodates slightly wide feet better than expected, but a dedicated wide version is not available at this time.
