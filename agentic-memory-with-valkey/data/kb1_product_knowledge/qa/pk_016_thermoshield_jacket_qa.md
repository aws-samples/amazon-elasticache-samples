# ThermoShield Running Jacket Community Q&A (Sample)

## Q1: Is this fully waterproof?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** ThermoShield has a DWR (durable water repellent) finish that sheds light rain and mist. It is not fully waterproof and will wet through in sustained heavy rain. For heavy rain, a full waterproof shell is recommended.

## Q2: Can I wear this for cycling?
**Answer (Community):** Yes, works great for cycling. The slim fit layers well and the reflective trim is helpful for road visibility. Thumb loops keep sleeves in place.

## Q3: How small does it pack?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** It packs into its own chest pocket to roughly the size of a softball. Easy to clip to a hydration vest or tuck in a waistband.

## Q4: Does the DWR wash out over time?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** DWR effectiveness decreases after approximately 20 washes. You can restore it by tumble drying on low heat or applying a spray-on DWR treatment.

## Q5: What temperature range is this good for?
**Answer (Community):** I use it from about 40°F to 55°F over a tee. Below 40°F I add a base layer underneath. Above 55°F it's too warm for running.
