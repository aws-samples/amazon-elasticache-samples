# HydroCarry Running Vest Community Q&A (Sample)

## Q1: Can I fit a phone in this?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** Yes. There are multiple stretch pockets on the front and sides that fit most smartphones. The front chest pockets are designed for soft flasks but side pockets accommodate phones.

## Q2: Does it come with a hydration bladder?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** HydroCarry includes two 500ml soft flasks. The rear sleeve fits a 1.5L hydration reservoir which is sold separately.

## Q3: Can I use this for hiking?
**Answer (Community):** Works great for hiking and fastpacking. I use it for day hikes up to 15 miles with plenty of storage for snacks, phone, and layers.

## Q4: How do I clean the soft flasks?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** Rinse soft flasks with warm water and mild soap after each use. For deep cleaning, use a bottle brush and baking soda solution. Air dry completely before storing. Do not put in dishwasher.

## Q5: Does it bounce when running fast?
**Answer (Community):** Almost zero bounce when the sternum straps are adjusted properly. Tighten them snug and it stays put even at tempo pace.
