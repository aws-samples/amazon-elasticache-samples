# SprintEdge Racer Community Q&A (Sample)

## Q1: Can I use this as a daily trainer?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** SprintEdge Racer is designed for race day and speed workouts. The thin outsole and minimal cushioning will wear quickly with daily use. For daily training, we recommend AeroFlex Runner 2 or TempoRun Interval Trainer.

## Q2: How many races will these last?
**Answer (Community):** I got about 5 races (mix of 10K and half marathon) plus some speed workouts before the outsole wore through. Maybe 150–200 miles total.

## Q3: Is the carbon plate noticeable?
**Answer (Community):** Very noticeable. You feel a distinct snap at toe-off. Takes a run or two to adjust your stride but then it feels amazing.

## Q4: Will a wide version be available?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** There are no current plans for a wide version of SprintEdge Racer. The narrow race fit is part of the performance design. Customers with wider feet may find TempoRun Interval Trainer a better option for speed work.

## Q5: Is this shoe legal for competitive races?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** Yes. SprintEdge Racer complies with World Athletics regulations for road racing shoes including stack height and plate specifications.
