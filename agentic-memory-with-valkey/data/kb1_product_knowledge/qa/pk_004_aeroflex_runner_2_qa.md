# AeroFlex Runner 2 Community Q&A (Sample)

## Q1: Does this shoe run small?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** Most customers find AeroFlex Runner 2 true to size in length. If you have a wide forefoot or prefer extra room in the toe box, consider AeroFlex Runner 2 Wide.

## Q2: Is this good for standing all day at work?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** Many customers use it for all-day wear and walking due to the lightweight build and balanced cushioning. If you prefer a softer, more cushioned feel for long standing periods, StrideMax Cushion Pro may be a better option.

## Q3: Can I use this for trail running?
**Answer (Community):** It is designed for road running and everyday use. For light trails and mixed terrain, TrailCore Grip 5 is the better option due to traction and toe protection.

## Q4: Is it machine washable?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** We recommend spot cleaning with mild soap and cold water and air drying only. Machine washing may affect shape and materials.

## Q5: What is the difference between AeroFlex Runner 2 and AeroFlex Runner 2 Wide?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** The Wide version uses the same platform and cushioning but has a roomier fit in the forefoot and toe box to better accommodate wider feet.
