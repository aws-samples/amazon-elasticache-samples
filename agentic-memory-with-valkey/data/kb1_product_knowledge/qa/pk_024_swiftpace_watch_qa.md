# SwiftPace Running Watch Community Q&A (Sample)

## Q1: Does it work with Strava?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** Yes. SwiftPace syncs with Strava, Garmin Connect, and other major running apps via Bluetooth. Activities upload automatically after syncing.

## Q2: Can I swim with it?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** SwiftPace is rated 5 ATM water resistant, suitable for swimming and showering. However, it does not have a dedicated swim tracking mode.

## Q3: How accurate is the GPS?
**Answer (Community):** After the latest firmware update, GPS accuracy is very good. My tracks match within 1–2% of my phone GPS on road runs. Slightly less accurate under heavy tree cover.

## Q4: Does it show phone notifications?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** No. SwiftPace is a dedicated running watch and does not display phone notifications, music controls, or apps. This is by design to minimize distractions during training.

## Q5: How long does the battery last in GPS mode?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** Up to 14 hours in continuous GPS mode. Sufficient for most ultra-marathon distances. In smartwatch mode (no GPS), battery lasts approximately 7 days.
