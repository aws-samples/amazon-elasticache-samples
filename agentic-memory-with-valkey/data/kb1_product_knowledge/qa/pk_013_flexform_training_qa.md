# FlexForm Training Shoe Community Q&A (Sample)

## Q1: Can I run in these?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** FlexForm is designed for gym training, lifting, and HIIT. The flat sole and minimal cushioning are not ideal for running. For running, we recommend AeroFlex Runner 2 or StrideMax Cushion Pro.

## Q2: Are these good for CrossFit?
**Answer (Community):** Yes, great for CrossFit. Stable for lifting, flexible enough for box jumps and rope climbs. The midfoot strap is clutch.

## Q3: How is the arch support?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** FlexForm has a flat, neutral platform by design for lifting stability. Customers who need arch support can add a custom insole.

## Q4: Do the soles mark gym floors?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** The rubber outsole is non-marking and safe for indoor gym floors.

## Q5: Is the midfoot strap necessary?
**Answer (Community):** You can tuck it away if you don't want it, but it really helps during lateral movements and heavy lifts. I always use it.
