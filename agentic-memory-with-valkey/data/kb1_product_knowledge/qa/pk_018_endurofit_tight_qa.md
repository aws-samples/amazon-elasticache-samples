# EnduroFit Compression Tight Community Q&A (Sample)

## Q1: Can I wear these for yoga?
**Answer (Community):** Yes, they work for yoga. The waistband can roll a bit in some poses but overall they're comfortable and supportive.

## Q2: What's the difference between these and regular leggings?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** EnduroFit uses graduated compression (firmer at calf, lighter at thigh) to support circulation during and after activity. Regular leggings typically have uniform stretch without compression benefits.

## Q3: Can I wear these in summer?
**Answer (Community):** They're warm. I only wear them below 50°F. In summer I switch to shorts.

## Q4: Why can't I use fabric softener?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** Fabric softener coats the fibers and reduces the compression properties and moisture-wicking ability of the fabric. Use cold water wash without softener for best performance and longevity.

## Q5: Do these have a phone pocket?
**Answer (Brand Rep):** Yes. There is a side pocket on the right thigh that fits most smartphones up to 6.7 inches.
