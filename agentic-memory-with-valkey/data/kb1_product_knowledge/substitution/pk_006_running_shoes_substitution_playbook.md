# Full Catalog Substitution Playbook (Support)

## Purpose
Help support agents recommend alternatives when a requested item is out of stock, unavailable in the customer's size, or mismatched for the intended use.

## Footwear Substitution Matrix

### AeroFlex Runner 2 unavailable
1. Check AeroFlex Runner 2 in another color
2. AeroFlex Runner 2 Wide if forefoot tightness reported
3. AeroFlex Walker if customer primarily walks (not runs)
4. StrideMax Cushion Pro if customer wants more cushioning
5. Do NOT recommend TrailCore Grip 5 unless trail use mentioned

### AeroFlex Runner 2 feels too firm
- Primary: StrideMax Cushion Pro ("softer underfoot feel for longer wear")
- Secondary: CloudWalk Recovery Slide for post-activity recovery

### AeroFlex Runner 2 feels too narrow
- Primary: AeroFlex Runner 2 Wide
- Secondary: AeroFlex Walker (slightly roomier last)

### StrideMax Cushion Pro unavailable
1. AeroFlex Runner 2 (less cushion but lighter)
2. AeroFlex Walker (similar comfort, walking-focused)

### TrailCore Grip 5 unavailable
1. TrailCore Grip 5 GTX (waterproof version; same fit)
2. Do NOT substitute road shoes for trail use

### TrailCore Grip 5 GTX unavailable
1. TrailCore Grip 5 (non-waterproof; same platform)

### SprintEdge Racer unavailable
1. TempoRun Interval Trainer (no carbon plate but similar speed-oriented design)
2. Do NOT recommend daily trainers as race shoe substitutes

### TempoRun Interval Trainer unavailable
1. SprintEdge Racer (faster but less durable)
2. AeroFlex Runner 2 (more cushion, less speed-oriented)

### FlexForm Training Shoe unavailable
1. No direct substitute in catalog; do not recommend running shoes for lifting
2. Suggest customer check back for restock

### CloudWalk Recovery Slide unavailable
1. No direct substitute; suggest any comfortable open-toe sandal

### ProGrip Performance Socks unavailable

## Recommendation Guardrails
- Do not describe water resistance unless explicitly listed in official product specs
- Do not promise medical/orthopedic benefits
- Do not guarantee fit; use "many customers find..." language
- Prioritize in-stock substitutions in same price band when possible
- Never recommend a race shoe (SprintEdge Racer) as a daily trainer

## Sample Agent Phrasing
- "If you want a similar everyday option, I can suggest a model with a slightly softer ride."
- "If toe-box room is the main issue, the wide version is usually the closest match before switching models."
- "For trail or gravel use, I would recommend a trail-specific outsole for better grip."
- "For gym and lifting, our training shoe has a flat stable base designed for that."
- "That race shoe is built for speed sessions; for daily training I'd recommend our interval trainer or daily runner."
