# Known Issue Resolved: AeroTrack Pro Charging Case Not Charging via USB-C
**Status:** Resolved
**Resolution Date:** 2026-01-15
**Severity:** Medium
**Affected Products:** AeroTrack Pro (early production units, serial numbers starting with AT24Q1)

## Original Issue
AeroTrack Pro charging cases from the first production batch (Q1 2024) would intermittently fail to charge via USB-C. Case LED would not illuminate when cable was connected. Earbuds inside would not charge.

## Root Cause
Defective USB-C port solder joint on early production PCBs. Thermal cycling (hot car, cold weather) caused the joint to crack over time.

## Resolution
- Hardware defect — no firmware fix possible.
- All affected serial numbers (AT24Q1-xxxxx) were eligible for free case replacement regardless of warranty status.
- Replacement program ran Oct 2024 – Jan 2026. **Program has ended.**
- Customers with AT24Q1 serial numbers who still have the issue: standard warranty replacement if within warranty. Out-of-warranty: offer replacement case at 50% discount ($24.99 instead of $49.99).

## Why This Document Exists
Retained for agents who may encounter late-arriving complaints about this issue. The replacement program is closed, but the discounted replacement offer remains available indefinitely.
