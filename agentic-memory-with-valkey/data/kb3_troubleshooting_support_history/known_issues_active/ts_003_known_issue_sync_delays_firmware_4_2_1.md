# Known Issue: Sync Delays After Firmware 4.2.1
**Status:** Active  
**Severity:** High  
**Effective Window:** 2026-02-18 to 2026-03-15 (subject to update)

## Summary
Some PulseFit Band 4 and PulseFit Band 4 Pro customers may experience delayed sync or repeated sync spinner behavior after updating to firmware version 4.2.1.

## Affected Scope
- PulseFit Band 4
- PulseFit Band 4 Pro
- Most commonly reported on iOS, but Android reports also observed

## Customer Symptoms
- Recent workouts appear after a long delay
- App shows "Syncing..." for extended periods
- Data eventually appears after multiple retries

## What Agents Should Do
1. Confirm firmware version (4.2.1)
2. Reassure customer that data may be delayed, not necessarily lost
3. Apply approved temporary workaround (see companion workaround note)
4. Avoid advising full app reinstall unless workaround fails and no outage is active
5. If customer is highly impacted (daily training use), create incident-linked case tag

## What Agents Should Not Do
- Do not promise a specific fix date
- Do not state data is permanently lost unless confirmed by engineering bulletin
- Do not use deprecated reconnect-loop workaround for this issue

## Resolution Status
Engineering investigation in progress. Updated guidance will be published in this KB when available.
