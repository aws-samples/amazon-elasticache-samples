**Customer:** Yeah it says v2.4.0, updated like 4 days ago

**Agent:** That explains it. We have a known issue with firmware v2.4.0 where the left earbud's noise cancellation microphone stays active even when ANC is turned off, which drains the battery faster than normal. Our engineering team has identified the fix and it's coming in the next firmware update.

**Customer:** Oh ok so it's not broken broken?

**Agent:** Correct — your hardware is fine. It's a software bug. In the meantime, I can help you with a workaround: go to the app → Sound Modes → and select "Off" instead of Transparency or ANC. This stops the mic from staying active and should bring your battery life back to normal.

**Customer:** What if I want to use ANC though?

**Agent:** Good question. If you need ANC, you can also downgrade to the previous firmware version. In the app, go to Devices → AeroTrack Pro → Firmware → Advanced → Install Previous Version. That'll take you back to v2.3.2 which doesn't have this bug. You'll get the fix in the next update automatically.

**Customer:** Ok doing that now. Thanks for knowing what this was right away, I was about to return them lol

**Agent:** Glad I could help! The fix should be out in early March. Is there anything else I can help with?
