# PulseFit Band 4 Initial Setup and Pairing Guide

## Purpose
Help customers set up PulseFit Band 4 and complete first-time pairing with the NorthPeak Health App.

## Before You Start
Make sure the customer has:
- A charged PulseFit Band 4 (at least 20% battery recommended)
- A supported iPhone or Android phone
- Bluetooth enabled on the phone
- Latest version of NorthPeak Health App installed

## First-Time Setup Steps
1. Power on PulseFit Band 4.
2. Open the NorthPeak Health App.
3. Sign in or create an account.
4. Tap **Add Device** and select **PulseFit Band 4**.
5. Allow Bluetooth permissions when prompted.
6. Confirm the pairing code shown on the band and phone.
7. Keep the app open until setup completes.

## If Pairing Fails
Try the following in order:
1. Confirm Bluetooth is enabled.
2. Move phone and band within 3 feet.
3. Force-close and reopen the app.
4. Restart the phone and retry pairing.
5. Restart the band and retry pairing.
6. Confirm app permissions were not denied (Bluetooth, local network if required by OS version, notifications optional).

## Common Setup Mistakes
- Customer exits app during firmware check
- Bluetooth permission was denied at first prompt
- Trying to pair from phone Bluetooth settings instead of the app
- Band battery too low to complete setup

## Agent Guidance
If customer reports repeated failure after permission denial on iPhone, search KB 3 for:
- pairing permission denial case patterns
- known issues tied to current app version
