# PowerBase Charging Dock Charging Interruption Troubleshooting

## Symptom
PulseFit Band charges intermittently or disconnects from charging after a few seconds when placed on PowerBase Charging Dock.

## Checks
1. Confirm dock is connected to approved power source
2. Inspect cable connection at dock and adapter
3. Confirm charging contacts on dock and band are clean and dry
4. Align band fully with charging pins/magnets
5. Remove any protective film or packaging residue from contacts

## Common Causes
- Misalignment on dock
- Dust/oil on charging contacts
- Low-output power adapter
- Protective film still covering dock contact plate (new units)

## Agent Guidance
If customer states "it charges only when I press it down," prioritize alignment and contact inspection before replacing device.
