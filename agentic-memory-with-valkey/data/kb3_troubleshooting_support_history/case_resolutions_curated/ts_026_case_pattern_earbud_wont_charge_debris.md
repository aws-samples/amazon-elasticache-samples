# Case Pattern: AeroTrack Pro One Earbud Won't Charge — Debris in Case Contact

## Pattern Summary
High-frequency case pattern (avg 12 cases/week). Customer reports one earbud (usually left) shows 0% battery and won't charge in case, while the other charges normally.

## Root Cause
Pocket lint, earwax, or debris accumulates on the charging contacts inside the case or on the earbud stem. The contact points are small (2mm) and even a thin layer of debris prevents charging.

## Resolution
1. Inspect charging contacts inside case (two gold pins per earbud slot). Look for visible debris.
2. Clean with dry cotton swab. For stubborn debris: lightly dampen swab with isopropyl alcohol (70%). Do NOT use water.
3. Clean earbud stem contacts (two gold rings near bottom of each earbud).
4. Place earbud in case — LED should illuminate within 5 seconds indicating charging.
5. If still not charging after cleaning: try the OTHER slot (swap L and R). If earbud charges in opposite slot, the original slot's contact pins may be bent. Escalate for case replacement.

## Prevention Advice
"To prevent this in the future, we recommend wiping the earbud stems and case contacts with a dry cloth once a week, especially if you use the earbuds during workouts."

## Stats
- First-contact resolution rate: 89%
- Avg handle time: 6 min
- Escalation rate: 11% (bent pins requiring case replacement)
