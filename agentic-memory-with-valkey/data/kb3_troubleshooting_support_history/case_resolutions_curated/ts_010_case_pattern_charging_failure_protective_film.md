# Case Pattern: Charging Failure Caused by Protective Film on Contacts

## Pattern Summary
New PowerBase Charging Dock units may appear defective when charging contacts are partially covered by clear protective film.

## Customer Phrasing Patterns
- "It only charges if I press it hard"
- "Dock light comes on but watch doesn't charge"
- "Brand new out of box and charging is inconsistent"

## Successful Resolution Path (Curated)
1. Ask customer whether dock is newly opened
2. Ask them to inspect dock contact surface for clear film or packaging residue
3. Remove film/residue
4. Re-seat band and confirm charging icon

## Escalate If
- No charge after contact cleaning and alignment
- Cable damage visible
- Customer reports dock overheating
